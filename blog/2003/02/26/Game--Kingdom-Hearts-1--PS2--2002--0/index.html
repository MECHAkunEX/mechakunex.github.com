Game: Kingdom Hearts 1 (PS2, 2002)

Feb 26th, 2003

In Kingdom Hearts, I’ve just about finished the game. I got to the “Final Rest” before the last boss and then I left and tried to tie up some loose ends. So far I’ve gotten all the synthesis items (and thus Sora’s ultimate weapon) and now I’m working on beating all the Coliseum cups alone & with a time limit. I kind of don’t want to finish the game because then I won’t have anything to play. Maybe I’ll cave in, beat it, and go buy something—not sure what though. I’m pretty happy I’m basically done and I started it last week. :)
