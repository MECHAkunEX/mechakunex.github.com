Feb 4th, 2012

They do a really good job communicating Serah’s changing feelings about her task… from meeting Lightning to saving the future. It is getting hard to keep track of what episode I am in. I maxed out my two main roles and should have gotten another role, but instead expanded my accessories. Oh well.
