Game: Iron Tank (NES, 1988)

Jan 10 2012
I played some of Iron Tank the other day. This was a super retro-goggle game. I didn't like it at all, but I remember playing the first level sooo many times as a kid. I found the controls to be really sloppy and the game to be too slow. This is definitely a common complaint I have, but I want games that are very exciting and fast. I don't have the patience for ones that are too mind-numbing.
