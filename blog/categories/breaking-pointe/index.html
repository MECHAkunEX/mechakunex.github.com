Breaking Pointe - Season 1 - Episode 6 - Curtain Call

Oct 22 2012
We finished the series tonight. I thought the final episode was pretty weak. I feel like the party for Katie was pretty contrived; the discussion of the final performances was really quickly cut, presumably because nothing happened; the meltdown between Allison and Rex was really annoying because Rex seems like such a jerk to her, but I'm glad that she reached out to him, because it seems like if she opens up, he'll be very good for her; and the thing between Christiana and her husband was touching.

Breaking Pointe - Season 1 - Episode 5 - Bad Dress: Good Show

Oct 21 2012
It was cool to watch this one, because we were at the opening show. I didn't notice anything wrong with Allison's dancing. I'm one of the "80% who don't know anything about ballet". It's kind of sad that she seems to have such contempt for ballet fans, like myself, who think it's all beautiful and can't notice flaws. I also felt bad for Allison when she talked with her family... she don't seem very supportive of her in the way she needs.
