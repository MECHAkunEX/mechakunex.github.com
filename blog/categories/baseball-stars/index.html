Game: Baseball Stars (NES, 1989)

Jan 11 2012
When I was young, I played Baseball Stars a lot with my friend Jeff, so I remembered it as being a great baseball game. When I just played it last night, I didn't really understand how to play it. I suppose I should have read the manual, but it was very non-intuitive and as I got a few innings in to the first game and started to understand it better, I realized that I would have liked it to be more RPG-like if I was going to play it alone. I imagine that playing with another person would have been a good experience, but by myself it really didn't catch me.
