Game: The Legend of Zelda: Link to the Past (SNES, 1991)

Aug 05 2012
I thought my son might like this game, given how much he enjoyed watching the beginning of Castlevania: SOTN. I also thought he might be willing to play it because the controls are simpler. I was right. He really enjoyed watching the beginning and then all of sudden he said, "Dad, how do you move? How do you attack? Can I try?" He explored the world a little and fought some enemies. He started getting frustrated because he kept dying. His problem was that he wouldn't move away from enemies when they came to attack him. He would swing his sword while the enemies were on top of him and this killed him eventually. We got to the boss of the first temple, but I died during the stomping part.
