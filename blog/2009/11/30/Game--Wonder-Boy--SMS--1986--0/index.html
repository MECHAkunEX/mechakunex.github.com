Game: Wonder Boy (SMS, 1986)

Nov 30th, 2009

Last night and tonight I played Wonder Boy for the Sega Master System. I used to play Adventure Island on my original NES, so when I learned that Wonder Boy was its root, I just had to play it.

This game destroyed me. It is so hard. There are so many times where your timing just has to be insanely perfect. I really enjoyed beating it though. I didn’t get all the dolls (so as I read on Wikipedia, I didn’t get to go to the 10th level.) I ended up with a score of 408,380. The high score for the MAME version is 2,122,630, but my score would be number 8; assuming that the SMS version is basically the same. (That tells me that it is not an active board.)

I used lots of save states and I don’t think I got all the dolls, i.e., didn’t get to level 10.
