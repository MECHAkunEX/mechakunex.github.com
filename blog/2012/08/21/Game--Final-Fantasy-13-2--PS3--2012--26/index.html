Game: Final Fantasy 13 2 (PS3, 2012) - DLC

Aug 21st, 2012

I wrote in my calendar to check on the DLC about six months after I beat the game, when presumably it would all have been released. It has. I looked for information about it and almost none of it seems interesting, except Lightning’s episode. But, I found that it only takes about 25 minutes (including cut-scenes) and that’s not worth 5 bucks, so I just watched it. The new ending is neat. I’m excited for 13-3. I love this battle system so much and I find the world to be really cool too. I hope it can be efficient for them to produce, make them lots of money, and make FF15 amazing.
