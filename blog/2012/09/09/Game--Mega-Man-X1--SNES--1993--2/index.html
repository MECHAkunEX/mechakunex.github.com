Game: Mega Man X1 (SNES, 1993) (3)

Sep 9th, 2012

This time I beat Chill Penguin, then went against Flame Mammoth (because I thought he’d be weak, but he wasn’t), then we went to Sting Chameleon’s stage and got the Armor upgrade. I’m not sure if I will use the password next time we play. Fighting the armor upgrade guard was te—-dious!
