Movie: 300, Directed by Zack Snyder (2007)

May 9th, 2012

This was an amazing movie. The visual were out of this world. The story is so inspiring. Although it is pretty bloody, for some reason there’s really only like two gross parts. The grossest thing was the disfigured people, but that’s weird, mainly because it’s actually real.

Looking at the guys in the movie makes you feel tiny, puny, and weak. It’s super intense.
