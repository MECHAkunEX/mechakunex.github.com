Game: Valkyria Chronicles (PS3, 2008) (13)

May 5th, 2012

I played for about four hours today, split across watching a movie. The battles were pretty tough. The once against Selvaria was insane, because she dodges practically everything. I eventually realized I should use a unit with the “Undodgeable Shot” ability, then it was all over. The one where you have to blow up the canyon walls, I kept doing it wrong because I didn’t realize there were two things to blow up to force it to turn. D’oh. I wasted like 30 minutes because of that.

I can’t believe that Welkin proposed to Alicia—he even had a ring! This game is so grown up compared to other RPGs, especially Japanese ones. The story is really cool, still.
