Game: Final Fantasy 13 2 (PS3, 2012) (8)

Feb 9th, 2012

Chocobo gliding! That’s how I got to the Graviton Beta! I just noticed the Paradigm save system—I wanted something like that in FF13.. I killed the Giant C’eith in Academia! It stinks he wasn’t guarding anything useful though. Searching through Academia for the core is horrible. Woah! Is Lightning a fal’Cie and she made Snow a l’Cie? I like that every temporal rift is different. I wrote a cute Racket program to solve the clock puzzle. I unlocked all the roles. I really like reading the stuff that comes with Fragments. It’s like the datalog from the first game. [023:53:55 Frags 49/160 Lvl 254]
