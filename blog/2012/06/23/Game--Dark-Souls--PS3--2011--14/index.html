Game: Dark Souls (PS3, 2011) (15)

Jun 23rd, 2012

I played for about three and a half hours today. I started by getting enough money to buy the Giant Armor and Havel’s Ring. Then I realized that if I wanted to upgrade the armor all the way, I’d need a lot of Twinkling shards, that were only sold by the guy I was planning on killing. After a little while grinding souls to buy the shards, I decided to check out Ash Lake where I could find an enemy that dropped them. Unfortunately, it was a major pain to get down the Great Hollow, plus the Black Hydra was really tough (and I couldn’t kill him…). When I finally go there, I only managed to get 2 drops in an amount of time I probably could have got enough souls to buy four or five. So I decided to leave… which took a lot of time to exit the Great Hollow. It was a major pain… and a major mistake to go down there. I think I’ll grind for souls and just buy them.
