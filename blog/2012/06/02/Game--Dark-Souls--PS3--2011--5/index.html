Game: Dark Souls (PS3, 2011) (6)

Jun 2nd, 2012

I played for like two hours today. I fought and won against Quelaag on my third or so time today. It was pretty epic to dodge her attacks and take her down. (But I used all five of my healing charges.) After taking care of business down there, I went back to near Sen’s Fortress and fought the demon underneath the blacksmith, upgraded my weapon a bit, and then started into the fortress. I died many times learning how to get through the first area and exploring it a bit. I feel like I have a good sense of the layout but not what I’m supposed to do in there. Maybe I need to drop down to the pit and do something there? More tomorrow, I guess.
