Game: Dark Souls (PS3, 2011) (9)

Jun 7th, 2012

Today I played for about three hours. I went through Anor Londo a little bit and got to the Painted World, which I completed. The wheel skeletons were pretty tough. I left the boss and the poison dragon thing for future attempts. When I got back in Anor, I pushed forward a bit. I got to the huge skeleton archers, but they knocked me off the ledge, so I died. I ended up losing 17k and 4 humanity that was on my body. I got back once but died again. On the second time I needed to get back, I got killed by the lightning drake things on the way. Well… not actually killed, I accidentally jumped off as I was trying to run away. It was really dumb. I love the way this game makes you feel dumb when you die.
