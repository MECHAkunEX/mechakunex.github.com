Game: Final Fantasy 7 (Bootleg Mods) (Steam, 1997)20131221
I took me a long time to install the Bootleg system after buying
this game on Steam. Once it was all ready, it was very hard to
decide which mods to use, because there isn't a lot of
description. I picked a good preset and tried to look into a few
of them.

I like the way of things look, particularly the UI. I like the new
soundtrack I chose. But, I think my favourite part is the speed of
everything. The new graphics are a little bit jarring because
everything doesn't have a consistent style (during a single scene)
like the game originally did. For instance, the "Advent Children"
style models walking next to the blocky Avalanche members look
really weird.

I'm enjoying the game so far too, I'm about to really get into the
second mission.
