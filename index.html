Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain (PS4, 2015) (32)20151125
I played today and finished the game, or at least all that I will
play. I did all the missions, did all the side-ops (that weren't
target practice, because those were really painful, since I
couldn't always find the targets), got all the tapes, and I
developed a huge amount of stuff. I didn't do all of the
objectives or get S-ranks on everything, but maybe some day I'll
play it again from the start and try to get them. My completion
percentage was 76%.

I saved Quiet's missions for the end, so I could use her in all
the other ones. I found the culmination of her story pretty
awesome and touching. I think she's one of the better characters
in a Metal Gear game.

Overall, I found this game to have the strongest game-play, really
great story moments, and amazing visuals. In terms of story, I
think my favourites are MGS3, MGS1, this game, followed by MGS4
and MGS2. In terms of story, it's this game, MGS3, MGS4, MGS1,
MGS2. I like it a lot. (I'm not sure how the other ones would fit,
Peace Walker is really good and Portable Ops is good too, although
not as good as PW.)

Total time: 94:48
