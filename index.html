Movie: Thor: Ragnarok, starring Chris Hemsworth (2017)20180121
This was a really fun movie. The fights were very spectacular and
the scenery was so intense. It was way funnier than previous movies
and I enjoyed it a lot. I was surprised to see Cate Blanchett in
the role, because the character seemed to be dressed and made-up to
be really youthful. It is weird that they switched out Heimdal's
actor and that they cast Karl Urban in such a minor role. I thought
the Valkyrie was a lot of fun, especially the awesome scene of them
fighting all together in the past. It was hilarious in the
beginning that such huge actors were in the play that Loki put
on. It really shows how great actors like Odin are when they can be
Odin pretending to be Loki pretending to be Odin. Surtur's
destruction of Asgard was really cool looking and kind of sad,
because it looks so awesome from afar.
