I played for a long time over a bunch of chunks today. I'm trying

paper. After that I went over to the Lecture Building and explored
the whole thing. I was about to go to the Nightmare Frontier, but
then I decided to do my first Chalice dungeon. I beat the first
boss on the first try, but the whole area was really easy given
how strong I am now.

This game is super awesome!
