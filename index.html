Game: Super Mario Maker (WiiU, 2015) (2)20180125
Since I originally got this game, I've played it a handful of
other times with my son. Today, I tried to sit down and find
levels to just play to get the experience of playing lots of great
Mario levels. I looked up lots of starred makers and levels and
did a few challenges, but I could not find a single level that was
not in one of these categories:
- Auto-playing
- Death fest
- Run to the right and watch what was going on below
- Completely easy and trivial

It was honestly one of the most disappointing things ever. I tried
to search online for lists of good levels that are actually worth
playing and couldn't find any. I think I will definitely keep it
around and encourage my kids to play making stuff, but the actual
playing of the game seems pretty bad. What a bummer.

So, my ranking of this game will be very low, because it reflects
its "fun-as-a-game". As a tool and experiment, I rate it very
highly though.

Total time: 1:00

Game: Tomb of Annihilation (Board, 2017) (5)20180125
Before GP: 1300gp

I played through adventure 6 and had a really good time. I could
have very easily died and nearly did so. I used a few nice
tricks. The used the Orb that the bard has to rearrange the
dungeon tile deck when I knew that I was almost to the final room,
which was really essential, because otherwise I would have been
swarmed by all the monsters. The caster was REALLY tough, yikes! I
got a few items, which appears to be the only thing that really
matters when it comes to treasure, although I definitely forgot to
draw treasure a few times. This game is really cool!

After GP: 2300gp

In between adventures, I decided to upgrade the Bard to level 2,
so now I've done that for all of them. I think next I will buying
the "advancements".

Before GP: 300gp

The next adventure was MUCH easier and pretty fast to go
through. I found a nice pattern to get the boss stuck in and it
was definitely a lot weaker than the last one.

After GP: 2300gp

I bought two recharge advancements and then one regain HP one. I
think these are going to be the most useful out of all the
options.

I am really enjoying this game a lot!

Movie: Thor: Ragnarok, starring Chris Hemsworth (2017)20180121
This was a really fun movie. The fights were very spectacular and
the scenery was so intense. It was way funnier than previous movies
and I enjoyed it a lot. I was surprised to see Cate Blanchett in
the role, because the character seemed to be dressed and made-up to
be really youthful. It is weird that they switched out Heimdal's
actor and that they cast Karl Urban in such a minor role. I thought
the Valkyrie was a lot of fun, especially the awesome scene of them
fighting all together in the past. It was hilarious in the
beginning that such huge actors were in the play that Loki put
on. It really shows how great actors like Odin are when they can be
Odin pretending to be Loki pretending to be Odin. Surtur's
destruction of Asgard was really cool looking and kind of sad,
because it looks so awesome from afar.
