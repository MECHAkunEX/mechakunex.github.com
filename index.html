Game: Bravely Default (3DS, 2014) (2)20140307
I played for about eight and a half hours again on a flight. I had
some fun boss fights. I was able to beat Othros in two rounds: the
first using Invigorate and then using Hidden Dragon. I used almost
the same strategy against the Summoner but because I knew the
fight was coming, I turned everyone into Monks and beat her in a
single round using just Hidden Dragons. 

On the other hand, when I fought the Time Mage & Sword Fencer and
Ranger, I didn't see the fight coming so I fought them with really
weak and weird jobs, like a Freelancer, Merchant, Thief, and Time
Mage.

I'm trying to get Level 9 on most of jobs. I did, however, just
decide to get Level 10 on Freelancer so I can get the JP%
increase.

Also, since I got the Summoner job, I need to go back to the Wind
Temple, where I noticed the Summoner guy. I'm currently partly
through Mt. Fragmentum.

I really enjoy the game still, but am definitely overpowered from
playing at 200% Encounter Rate. For instance, when I switched to
Chapter 2, everyone was level 36 and I had two jobs each at level
9, plus one other job at level 6.

Something I really like about the game, but am surprised about, is
that when you beat the job enemies they really die. It's intense.
