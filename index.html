Game of Thrones - Season 5 - Episode 10 - Mother's Mercy (2)20160727
This was a very depressing episode with things getting worse for
almost everyone. I think it is very telling that Melissandre is
excited about the thaw, as though she wasn't sure it would really
happen. It's sad that Brienne missed Sansa's signal... I wonder
if she'll ever tell her. Arya's attack on Trant was awesome, but
it is very sad that she's now blind. It's cute that Tyrion and
Varys are now best buds teaming up. It's ridiculous that Ellaria
kills Mrycella just when she has a great bonding moment with
Jaime. Cersei's walk was very intense and it is interesting to
realize that she is now totally determined to destroy every last
one of them. It is telling that Davos' first question is about
Shireen. It's cool that Sam will be a maester. The mutiny of the
Night's Watch was crazy.
