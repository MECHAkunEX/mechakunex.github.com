Game: Monster World 4 (GEN, 1994)20130814
I played this for about an hour and beat the first "level" and got
to the Monster World. This is definitely a huge improvement over
the previous games in the series and pretty great for a Genesis
game, so I can see why it made such a big impression on people,
big enough to be mentioned as a "great game" of the past.

I'd say that despite that, it just isn't good enough compared to
other games in the genre to warrant spending a lot of time with
it. Its combat is much less interesting than a Castlevania game,
for instance, which shares a lot of the other structure with it.
