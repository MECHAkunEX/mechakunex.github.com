Movie: The Dark Knight Rises, directed by Christopher Nolan (2012) (2)20141229
We watched this to close out the series. It's very slow paced in a
lot of different ways than the previous movies. The fights, the
plot, etc. It also feels quite disconnected from the earlier movies
in terms of people's personalities: in particular, I don't find it
believable that Batman would be cozy with Catwoman at the end. It
sucks hardcore that Bruce lost everything. Nevertheless, it was
nice finish to the trilogy.

I think it is awesome the way they set up Robin taking over for
Batman. He's disgusted at himself for accidentally killing those
guys, disgusted at the police for following stupid orders, etc.
