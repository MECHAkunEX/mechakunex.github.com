Game: Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy 13 (PS3, 2014) (18)20140309
I played for about four hours today and got the rest of the
trophies. At first, I just wandered around the world and did
stuff, for about three hours. Then I realized that if I just
wanted to get the trophies and not beat the game again, I could
just go get accessories. So I switched and did that. It only took
about 30 minutes more after that. I restarted and got all the
accessories again in just an hour. It was nice to complete
everything. I don't feel like the Pallas Athena garb is great
though.

Overall, I love this game. I think I prefer it to FF13-2, but not
FF13-1. This series is just so good. The battle system is amazing
and the story is so nuts. I like that this game doesn't have any
mindless grindy parts. Even fighting the Last Ones is really fun
because they are bosses. I think the main disadvantage of this
game versus the others is that there aren't as many bosses or
crazy enemies. Still, the combat is so amazing.

decide to get Level 10 on Freelancer so I can get the JP%

Temple, where I noticed the Summoner guy. I'm currently partly

I really enjoy the game still, but am definitely overpowered from
