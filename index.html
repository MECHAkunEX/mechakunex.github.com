Game: Dark Souls: The Board Game (Board, 2017)20170507
My son and I played a round of this going against the
Gargoyles. It was pretty fun. I found my characters to be almost
totally useless against the enemies. It felt like there wasn't
enough strategy, just luck. I found the souls to be kind of
pointless, because you need to level up just to use equipment, but
the equipment isn't guaranteed to be any better (we pulled about
10 pieces and didn't get anything we could use or wanted.) I think
there should be less randomness to make a more Dark-Souls-like
experience. We had fun though and the minis were beautiful.

Total time: 2:00
