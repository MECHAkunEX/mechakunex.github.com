Movie: Mother's Day, directed by Garry Marshall (2016)20170310
This was a poorly designed movie with no heart. It was like a
sketch comedy movie, but with emotional sketches and
one-dimensional characters. A few of the scenes were really bad,
like when Britt decides to confront her mom. The world of the movie
ignores a lot of reality. Nevertheless, it satisfies in the moment
and is not a waste of two hours.
