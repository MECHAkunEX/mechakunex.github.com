Game: The Fool's Errand (PC, 1987)20170709
The Wheel of Fortune --- Annoying to play a game when you don't
know the rules of the game. I suppose I could write down the
match-ups that I lose/win and maybe write a program to tell me
what the best strategy would be.

The World --- This is much easier than others. The last four were
very difficult---Norway, Rumania, Albania, and Italy.

The Maze of Hedges --- It seems like the Ws don't always appear in
the same places.

Strength --- This one was fun.

Temperance --- This was weird.

The Canopy --- Another word search! It was much easier.

The Fighting Boys --- I wrote a Racket program to solve this one.

The Hanged Man --- Picture puzzle.

The Page of Cups --- I used grep for this one to search for
anagrams.

The Emperor --- This was a hard one. The last few words were very
hard to find. I didn't know what a Whippoorwill was. I gave up and
couldn't find the last word.

This is a very complicated game. Each individual puzzle is quite
basic, but the depth of game is intimidating.

Total time: 3:01
