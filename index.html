Book: Nightfall, by Isaac Asimov (1990)20150704
I started this book today. It is very interesting. From what I can
tell, it is a world where because there are four stars in the
system, the world is always alight, although some of the stars are
not as bright, so they do have a concept of it being dark. This
means that they have never seen any distant stars, which is a very
strange thing to imagine what that would be like. It makes me
wonder if they'd be able to do stuff like navigate or learn about
gravity... things which the stars had a lot of impact on in our
history. It's very interesting. I presume that the plot will be
that "nightfalls" but I have no idea how or why that would happen.
