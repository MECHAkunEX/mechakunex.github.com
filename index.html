Movie: Resident Evil: Retribution, starring Milla Jovovich (2012)20130105
This movie was nuts, big, and stupid like all the other Resident
Evil movies. The setup is crazy, absurd, and retcons everything of
the past ones... for example, apparently every star of the past
movies is a clone! (This is to be able to have tons of cameos.) My
favourite part of the clone thing is when Becky says to Michelle
Rodriguez #3 that she met "her sister (and she wasn't very
nice)". Hah!

It's brilliant and so dumb that they were able to create a video
game-style "level" setup with the various test sites. Strangely,
it is so reminiscent of the first one, it's one of the best. I
think Alice's care for Becky is sweet.

The final sequence is insane and Wesker is ridiculous and annoying
as usual. I wonder what will happen next?

This review blurb is great: ``Clark Collis from Entertainment
Weekly also gave the film a mostly positive review, stating that
"writer-director W.S. Anderson's overseeing of the Resident Evil
zombie franchise has proven to be both lunatically haphazard and
dementedly enthusiastic."''

Movie: Total Recall, starring Colin Farrell (2012)20130104
This was a really cool movie. I liked the original and I thought
this was a great modernization. It did a very good job of having
many layers of memories and doubt at what his true personality was.

Some little things:
- The Fall was very cool
- At one point near the end of the movie, Quaid refers to the name
of Jessica Biel's character and even though it's the end of the
movie, this is the first time we've heard her name.
- Jessica Biel is convincing as an action person, although she
doesn't have enough practice as Kate Beckinsale.
- Beckinsale looks great, as usual. My wife noticed how everything
about her character is to be super sexy. She can't stand still or
talk on the phone without pouting or standing
seductively. Whereas Biel puts her hair in a pony-tail when they
are chased, Beckinsale leaves her hair down so it looks better. I
don't think these things are terrible, but it is interesting.

The worst thing about Beckinsale's character is the ridiculously
bad lines she's given. They are bad for an 80s star: "I give
good wife." (This makes no sense, is out of context in the
scene, and is just there to make you think of her doing
something else.) "Do you think I'd let you leave without one
last kiss?" and many lines like it that are lame 50s Spider
Man/Batman quips.
- Seeing the three-breasted woman was a surprise given the rating.

Overall, I enjoyed the movie a lot.

Game: Bangai-O HD: Missile Fury (X360, 2011) (5)20121230
This morning I played for a little more than an hour. I just did
two stages.

Stage 32 --- This was a frustrating level... not really that hard,
but it was very difficult to control your fire, since the rest of
the game is designed around shooting tons and tons of bullets. In
this level, the ceiling can collapse if you touch it, blocking you
in from the rest of the level. You have to be very controlled in
your fighting. It was definitely interesting though.

Stage 33 --- This was a very fun "arena"-style level with three
phases of enemies. First, there's a maze of hedgehogs and their
generators. Then, a bunch of spinning things appear with some
support. (A few ninjas too!) Finally, a bunch of Napalm-shooting
tanks and Longai-O (?) appear. It was very challenging to deal
with everything, although I'd say the first and last phase were
the hardest. But, you got so much practice with the first one, it
felt easy at the end.
