Game: Bravely Default (3DS, 2014) (13)20140403
Final bosses that are introduced in the last moments of the game
are a staple of JRPGs that is hard to understand, because you
don't really care about defeating them, since they have had no
impact on the game.

It's neat that they justify the cross-game features in the end.

The rip in space at the end was fun. I only had one character die
in final battle... but I wouldn't say it was a close call. I
stupidly used a Megalixir rather than just use Curaga. Otherwise,
it was a very clean fight. 

The final song is cute.

Overall, I really enjoyed this game. The battle system is fun and
I love that even after you're totally maxed out, it is still
difficult, yet fun to figure out ways to exploit it.

The final movie was cool. I like the way the title screen now says
"Airy Lies". Neat!

Total time: 54:15
