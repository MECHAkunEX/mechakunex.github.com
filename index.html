Game: Environmental Station Alpha (Steam, 2015) (9)20151217
I played today for about two hours and ended up beating the
game. There were a few really weird things. I managed to beat it
without going to an area that was blinking and I never could
figure out how to get there. I wandered randomly for a long time
and eventually found a way to get to a super powered upgraded. It
was really hard to get it, but it was awesome. The ending of the
game was pretty sad. It makes me curious if there is a different
way to beat the game and get a "good ending". I think the game
plays really well and the bosses are very fun. I think the
traversal of the world is not great though, because there are so
many dead-ends, one-way drops, and inconveniences. While it is fun
to play the game, it is not fun to plan your trip and get where
you want to go. Unlike SoTN, for instance, the enemies generally
just swarm you and it's not a fun little "boxing" match to take
them down. In summary, I definitely enjoyed the game, but I don't
love it and don't expect to play it again.

Total time: 12:53
