Game: BIT.TRIP RUNNER (Steam, 2011) (2)20140118
I played today for about an hour and beat the other two
levels. The beginning of this game was pretty fun and unique, but
as the levels get longed, it ceases to be as fun, because it feels
like it just about memorizing a pattern. The "boss" fight is the
worst because you cannot anticipate what the enemy is going to
drop on you and you really need to just straight-up memorize
it. One person on my friends list had played the game and given up
at that point, so I imagine a lot of people get turned off
there. I did the first level of Zone 2 and saw that they added
some mechanics but it felt too painful already, so I didn't go
through it.

A good, but flawed, game.

Game: Recettear: An Item Shop's Tale (Steam, 2010)20140114
I played this game for about an hour. I got passed the first week
with a huge profit and got my adventurer's level up a lot. The
game is pretty cute, but too basic for my taste. It is basically
just about grinding, but the mechanics of battles aren't
particularly good compared to best-in-class action RPGs. 

I don't regret spending an hour with the game, but I do regret
spending $5 on it during the Steam sale. I think the subject
matter is very cute and the concept is very unique, but neither is
strong enough to support the bland mechanics.
