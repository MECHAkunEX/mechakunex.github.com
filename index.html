Movie: The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones (2013)20131130
This is yet another movie trying to be the heir of Harry
Potter. This one is distinguished by being focused on a young
woman---in a way reminiscent of Twilight. Furthermore, it starts
with the grittiness and violence that Harry Potter ended with. The
movie has a lot of plot in it... I felt like there were many
opportunities for it to be finished with exposition and just move
on, but it didn't. Despite all this plot, there were some weird
unexplained things: like why did Bane show up in the Institute?
What happen to Simon being a vampire? There were a lot of hokey
moments---like the rain during the kiss. But overall, it was pretty
enjoyable.

I really disliked the moment of ironic sexism when Clary says, "Why
will it help my mom if I dress like a hooker?" We're supposed to
say, "Isn't she smart for saying that?" But the movie still puts
her on display and doesn't react to her, except for the guy (who
turns out to be her brother) who says, "Actually you look great",
which causes Clary to blush. Thus, we learn that boys like you when
you dress like a hooker. Great.

Game: Illusion of Gaia (SNES, 1993) (5)20131129
I finished the game today. It was so much fun and I'm so impressed
by the little things about the game. The music and graphics are
great. The gameplay is really good. The story and moments are
cute.

I found the Mt. Temple map very confusing. It was very endearing
and touching how Hamlet sacrificed himself and then talking to the
tribe afterwards was cool.

I love that you get Shadow. He's so rad, but he doesn't totally
break the game. The Mummy Queen especially is really hard. She was
much harder than the final boss for me. I died like 8 times on the
second round with her. The second round with the Vampires was hard
too.

I was so annoyed at how slow the text moved at the end of the
game. Blah.
