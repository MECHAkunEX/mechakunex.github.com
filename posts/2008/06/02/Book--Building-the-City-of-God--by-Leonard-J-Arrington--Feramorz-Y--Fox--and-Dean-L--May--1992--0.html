Book: Building the City of God, by Leonard J Arrington, Feramorz Y. Fox, and Dean L. May (1992)20080602
<p>This book starts with a history of the consecration movement in
Joseph Smith's time, then follows the development of ZCMI and other
early *REDACTED* efforts, such as the Brigham City cooperative, then
reviews (in great detail) the history of the United Order
movement---focusing on individual orders of interest--from its
start to its decline, finally there is a discussion of Zion's Board
of Trade and the development of the modern Welfare plan. The topics
are very interesting in general, but the detail can be a bit
tiresome for the casual reader.</p>

<p>If you are interested in these subjects, I'd suggest checking
out the first and last chapters on the topic of interest. The
material in between can easily defeat you.</p></div>
