Game: Tokyo Jungle (PS3, 2012) (3)20171220
I played through the Chick's level and then tried to do the Rabbit
but died instantly. The Chick really sucked because it moved so
slow. I also died the next story stage.

I love this review: Ellie Gibson, writing for Eurogamer, gave
Tokyo Jungle a 9/10, describing it as "basically Grand Theft Auto
with lions". and calling it "a celebration of classic games, with
their ridiculous plots, repetitive tasks, excessive violence and
all. It pulls off the impressive and nigh-on impossible trick of
being an original homage. Also it lets you set a giraffe on a
bear."

But despite this, it is really painful to play and the fighting is
not that great. The theme is so amazing though.
