Game: Tabletop Simulator - Kingdom Death: Monster (Board, 2016) (2)20171226
I spent about forty minutes reading about what to do next. I'm
trying to decide whether to fight a level 1 or a level 2 White
Lion next.

--

I decided to go with a level two. I'm very nervous, but I lucked
out during the hunt. I got Clean Water (bonus), Lion's Sculpture
(bonus), Frozen Lightning (one person got hurt, Arya), then I was
ambushed in the Sea of Golden Grass. I'm glad to have been
ambushed though, because it means fewer Hunt Events to get hurt
during. I got quite a lot of interesting terrain, too.

So, next time I play, I'll have to deal with the actual
Showdown. Eeeee! It's cool how exciting it is.
