Game: Dungeon of the Endless (Steam, 2014)20170721
I played three rounds of this. I died on the 1st, 3rd, and 2nd
floor. It is an interesting concept for a game. It's a mix of so
many different genres. I feel like I need to stop getting random
heroes and choose them more thoughtfully. I think another good
strategy might be to open a door and then always run back towards
the crystal, which I have reinforced with some attack pods. The
game is such high stress and risk, I'm at the point in my
understanding of it that I can't believe it's not totally random.
