Game: Dungeon of the Endless (Steam, 2014) (2)20170723
I read a tutorial and then played the game for about an hour and
pretty well crushed it. When I first played, I assumed that it
would be more important to have a lot of industry generators,
because that seemed like it would be better for the long term, but
I didn't realize how short the levels would be. The tutorial
suggested that food was much better, because you could level up
your characters and that would last the whole game, since you were
not reset from floor-to-floor, like modules were. I think this has
been the key.

The problem, however, is that so far I have built literally
nothing but food generators and a few of the pea shooters. It
seems strange that the game would have so much more content
(i.e. other things to build) but have them not actually be
useful. Either the game is stupid or I am stuck in a local maxima
and it will come to hurt me as I get more deep into the game.

I am presently on floor five with four characters.
