I went through Vector and have the freedom to drive the airship
wherever I want. I'm annoyed that I can only bank 30 SP. It is

because Locke and Terra were so weak, but Strago's Aqua Rake is
awesome.

the plot, like Celes being a spy and Shadow being related to Relm
that I never saw before, but maybe it is just this translation and
