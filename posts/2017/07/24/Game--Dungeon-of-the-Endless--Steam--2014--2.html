Game: Dungeon of the Endless (Steam, 2014) (3)20170724
Floor 7, Held off elevator attack for long time

Floor 8, Lots of close calls.

Floor 9, A hard layout and very little dust.

Floor 10, This was really hard because I kept having my crystal
attacked and it just snowballed a lot.

Floor 11, I explored a lot of the floor, but left after running
out one direction and having no more dust to power that side of
the floor.

Floor 12, I went the wrong direction, but then ran out as I found
the exit and won! Woot.

--

I had fun with this game, but can't really imagine wanting to play
it again. The game makes a lot of sense and is pretty
straight-forward to play. I can tell that they tried very hard to
add lots and lots of other things to it so that there would be
depth to it, but I didn't find anything other than Food and the
dinky offensive Pods to be useful at all. I don't have much of an
interesting in trying the other things, because it would just seem
like an artificial constraint.
