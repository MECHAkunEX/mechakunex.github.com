Movie: Beauty and the Beast, starring Emma Watson (2017)20170520
The summary on IMDb for this is "An adaptation of the fairy tale
about a monstrous-looking prince and a young woman who fall in
love", but it is actually a shot-for-shot remake of the animated
movie. I thought that I would like this, and there's definitely
some places where it is great, like seeing the beautiful dresses
and crushing on a real woman. On the other hand, it is a bit too
bland and everything is just expected, like I've watched the movie
a million times. That's a strange experience for the first time
you've ever seen something. While I was "in" the movie, I enjoyed
it a lot, but I think it is too derivative to be a classic.
