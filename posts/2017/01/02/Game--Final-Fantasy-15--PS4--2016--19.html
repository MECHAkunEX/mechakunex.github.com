Game: Final Fantasy 15 (PS4, 2016) (20)20170102
I played for three hours today and for the first part I continued
the story. The story is insane... it's nuts who the emperor became
and what happened with the crystal. The "World of Ruin" was a nice
throwback and really crazy storywise. I really liked seeing the
Demon Wall, although I couldn't put a dent in it. The amount of
demons roaming around the world is very intimidating. I went back
in time once I got control and did a bunch of quests. It was weird
that the bros didn't have more reconnecting, but I assume they
will just drip-feed it.
