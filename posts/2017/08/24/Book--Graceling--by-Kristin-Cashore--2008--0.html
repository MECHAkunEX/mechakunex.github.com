Book: Graceling, by Kristin Cashore (2008)20170824
My family listened to this for a while, but there was something
wrong with the audio file, so we stopped around chapter 24. Today,
I started it from there and listened a lot.

I enjoy the world and how it has a very lived-in feel, despite
having super heroes. I like Katsa's personality and how you get to
know her very well. The sex scene was... interesting, especially to
listen to with an 8, 6, and 4 year old! The action is nice and
intense.
