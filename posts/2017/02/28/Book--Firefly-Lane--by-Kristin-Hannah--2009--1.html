Book: Firefly Lane, by Kristin Hannah (2009) (2)20170228
I'm 22% in and I think the book is short-shifting Kate in the same
way that Kate thinks the world does. Tully is clearly set up as the
main character and Kate doesn't have much of an identity. In
particular, there's very little connection between the way she acts
as a college student and how she was before. It makes me wonder if
Tully was ultimately bad for her as kids... the author hasn't
really sold Kate getting something from the relationship.

--

I can't believe Tully didn't feel like she was being abused by the
professor. Ugh.
