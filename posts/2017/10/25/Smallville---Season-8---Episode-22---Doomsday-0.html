Smallville - Season 8 - Episode 22 - Doomsday20171025
It was unfortunate that they didn't actually show the final fight
with Clark and Doomsday. I wonder if they just ran out of
money. I don't really believe that he would stay trapped in the
tunnel, but I'm also skeptical they will be lazy enough to bring
him back. The introduction of Zod was nice. It was insane that
Davis and Jimmy both died, but necessary from a writing
perspective to make Chloe interesting again.

I can't believe Season 8 ended and we still had no flying or
Superman. Ugh. I also don't like that it was never explained
where Tess actually came from. I ended up really liking her and
feeling pretty good about her intentions, as a character.
