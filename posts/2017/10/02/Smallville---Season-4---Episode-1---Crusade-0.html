Smallville - Season 4 - Episode 1 - Crusade20171002
This was a very intriguing opening, but I am quite annoyed that
so many of the details are immediately dropped for small town
drama. Big questions for the season:
- What is the tattoo? (Some mystical prophetic mark.)
- What happens when all three artifacts come in? (Presumably it
opens up Kryptonian knowledge to Clark.)
- What is black Kryptonite? (A dumb deus ex machina.)

I was extremely disappointed in Lois Lane's existence. My
confidence in Clark and Chloe coming together is more and more
low now. But, Lana and Lex's stock is rising.

I was also really disappointed that they are doubling down on
"Jor-El and natural Kryptonians are evil."
