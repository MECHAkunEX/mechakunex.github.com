Game: Sakura Arms (Board, 2017)20180414
I played the example decks against myself. It was really cool. The
second player would have lost because they had to reshuffle their
deck and had one life left. On one hand, that feels like a lame
way to die, but I did discard from her a lot, so it is definitely
my fault. I played through their turn though to see what I would
have done and they were able to kill the first player. This is
cool because it means that the two initial decks are really well
balanced if I could basically beat either one with the other.

Wow, the cards are SO beautiful and the style of the game is
amazing. I love this. Now, how can I convince my wife to learn how
to play?
