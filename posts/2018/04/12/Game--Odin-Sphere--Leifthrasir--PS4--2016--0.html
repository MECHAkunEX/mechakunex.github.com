Game: Odin Sphere: Leifthrasir (PS4, 2016)20180412
I started this and did three chapters of the Valkyrie story. This
game is great. I really enjoy the improvements from the PS2
version. (I rented the PS2 one but had a really hard time playing
because of the need of harvesting plants during the battle.) I
think that mechanics of Muramasa are slightly better, but the
variety of this, especially in the different skills and characters
is really great. I'm having a blast so far!
