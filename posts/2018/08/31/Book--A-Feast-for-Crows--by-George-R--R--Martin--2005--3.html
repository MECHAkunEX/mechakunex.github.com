Book: A Feast for Crows, by George R. R. Martin (2005) - Chapter 13 & 14 & 15 & 1620180831
The Kingsguard of Myrcella brings so much more character to her. I
found her enjoyable in her two episodes in the show, but there
wasn't much substance to hold on to. I really don't believe that
the woman actually likes this guy though.

Brienne is on such a wild goose chase. It is a nice realistic
fleshing out of the world though that she is so totally helpless in
this big quest.

I find it very surprising that Sam doesn't want to be a
maester... it seems like right up his alley. It was cool to hear
about the weird Skaagos place. "I dream I was old"! What a great
line. I find the idea of the swapped babies to be really
terrible... presumably Jon will reveal to Melissandre that he did
it so she won't kill Gillie's baby.

Jaime is so disgusted with Cersei and she really earns. All the new
factions of Lannisters and the way she is trying to make her own
council is so much more stupid in this. In the show, she seems
clever.
