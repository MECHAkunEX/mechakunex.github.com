Book: A Storm of Swords, by George R. R. Martin (2000) - Chapter 56 & 57 & 58 & 59 & 6020180813
Bran's chapters are so much fun because we learn a lot more about
the history of the world. I loved how scared they were of Sam. I
wonder if Coldhands will be on the other side, that's different!
The Black Gate was awesome too.

Dany's chapter was not quite as awesome as the show. I loved it
when she threw the chains in. She has many more advisers in the
book and the early reveal of Jorah & Barristan is interesting.

Tyrion's thoughts about Shae are so sad and pathetic. I like the
more noble version of him that doesn't see her after he's married.

Sansa's take on the gifts and Joffrey being terrible is always
great. I like that while Tyrion is trying to be good to her, she's
trying to figure out what trick he's playing and what she
wants. Yet another of the many examples of misunderstandings and
mistakes that condemn the people in this world.

Tyrion's theory about Joffrey trying to kill Bran is interesting. I
love how Olena "fixes" Sansa's hairnet. Yikes! He is so so
terrible.
