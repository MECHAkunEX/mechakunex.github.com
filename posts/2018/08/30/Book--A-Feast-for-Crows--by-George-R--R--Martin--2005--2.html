Book: A Feast for Crows, by George R. R. Martin (2005) - Chapter 9 & 10 & 11 & 1220180830
I was very surprised when Brienne ran into Podrick! It seems quite
brave of him to go after her like that!

Sansa's chapter made Robin seem even more pathetic than the show. I
love Sansa's training from Petyr.

Asha's code name of the "Kraken's Daughter" was rad. She is so
tough. I love her confidence about the Queensmoot and how she
treated the "love sick puppy".

Cersei's paranoia about Tyrion is ridiculous. Her disdain for
everyone around her, even Jaime is crazy too. I like the slight
hints of the Woods Witch's prophecy that we get.
