Game: Descent: Journeys in the Dark 2nd Edition (Board, 2012) (6)20180127
We had our second session and just did the Castle Daerion
quest. We made one big mistake in the first encounter: I thought
that I could not spawn reinforcements if a player was blocking the
spot, but I then read that you could. That would have changed
things a lot. In the second encounter, I swarmed the objective and
completely annihilated him in three turns. It seemed really
unfair.

It was a lot of fun in any case. There were a lot of tense rolls
and strategic moments. I think I have one super-player that wants
to dominate what everyone else does and some people are clearly
annoyed about it. A few of the players were really checked out and
spending a lot of time looking at their phones. It was pretty
annoying for me actually, but oh well, I'm doing it so that they
can have a fun night and bond as young men, not just to play the
game. I feel like I could faithfully play this game by myself
though and make the most strategically difficult decisions for
myself.
