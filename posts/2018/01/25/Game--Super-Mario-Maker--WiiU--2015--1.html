Game: Super Mario Maker (WiiU, 2015) (2)20180125
Since I originally got this game, I've played it a handful of
other times with my son. Today, I tried to sit down and find
levels to just play to get the experience of playing lots of great
Mario levels. I looked up lots of starred makers and levels and
did a few challenges, but I could not find a single level that was
not in one of these categories:
- Auto-playing
- Death fest
- Run to the right and watch what was going on below
- Completely easy and trivial

It was honestly one of the most disappointing things ever. I tried
to search online for lists of good levels that are actually worth
playing and couldn't find any. I think I will definitely keep it
around and encourage my kids to play making stuff, but the actual
playing of the game seems pretty bad. What a bummer.

So, my ranking of this game will be very low, because it reflects
its "fun-as-a-game". As a tool and experiment, I rate it very
highly though.
