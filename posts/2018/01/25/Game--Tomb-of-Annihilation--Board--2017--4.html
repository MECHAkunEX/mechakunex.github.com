Game: Tomb of Annihilation (Board, 2017) (5)20180125
Before GP: 1300gp

I played through adventure 6 and had a really good time. I could
have very easily died and nearly did so. I used a few nice
tricks. The used the Orb that the bard has to rearrange the
dungeon tile deck when I knew that I was almost to the final room,
which was really essential, because otherwise I would have been
swarmed by all the monsters. The caster was REALLY tough, yikes! I
got a few items, which appears to be the only thing that really
matters when it comes to treasure, although I definitely forgot to
draw treasure a few times. This game is really cool!

After GP: 2300gp

In between adventures, I decided to upgrade the Bard to level 2,
so now I've done that for all of them. I think next I will buying
the "advancements".

Before GP: 300gp

The next adventure was MUCH easier and pretty fast to go
through. I found a nice pattern to get the boss stuck in and it
was definitely a lot weaker than the last one.

After GP: 2300gp

I bought two recharge advancements and then one regain HP one. I
think these are going to be the most useful out of all the
options.

After GP: 0gp

I am really enjoying this game a lot!
