Game: Tomb of Annihilation (Board, 2017)20180110
I played the first adventure with three characters and it took two
hours, including setup, clean up, and reading the rules. It was
fun and pretty tense. I was expecting there to be like a "boss" at
the end, so I saved my Daily powers longer than I should have. I
used both Healing Surges and it was really exciting. There wasn't
as much strategy as I'd hoped, it is more about mitigating risk
and avoiding putting yourself out there.

I played with three players and was surprised that there were no
rules for balancing, but then after playing for a while, I
realized that more players will naturally lead to more enemies
being discovered and/or more encounters happening. I never had it
happen where two players at the same monster card, but I was quite
nervous about it, because that would really be painful. It is very
interesting that the monsters "teleport" into your spot. It makes
it so there's no way to cheese their AI by moving them around in a
stupid way.

So far, I like the game!
