Book: The Dark Tower, by Stephen King (2004) (3)20180216
I got to the Epilogue. The ending confrontation with the Crimson
King (and Mordred) were really disappointing. It was also
disappointing that nothing happened in the Tower... I wonder if the
Epilogue will make it worth it? I doubt it.

--

I finished everything and ya, it is a big let-down. I understand
what King is saying at the end, but still I found the conclusion of
the story to be really terrible. There's like no point to anything
and I feel like the story doesn't "mean" anything. In some ways it
is more like a traditional myth in that a bunch of stuff happens
that is all disconnected and weird. I suppose Roland changes a
little bit in becoming more attached to people, but not
significantly so.
