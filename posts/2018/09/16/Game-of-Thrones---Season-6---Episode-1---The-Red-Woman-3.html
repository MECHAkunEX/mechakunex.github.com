Game of Thrones - Season 6 - Episode 1 - The Red Woman (4)20180916
I'm surprised there is not more consequence right away for
Thorne. I love that Brienne is good but not perfect in her
fighting. I love that she immediately offers her service and how
Sansa accepts in such a humble circumstance. I'm surprised there
is no consequence at all for the Dornish guards and it seems
implausible that the Sand Snakes could get to King's Landing so
quickly. "It's a good thing you're not a boy anymore." "I want to
see what the world looks like when she's done conquering it." I
feel like Melissandre is checking to see if the Lord of the Light
is really real after being disappointed by Jon's death. I don't
get the impression she does this every night.
