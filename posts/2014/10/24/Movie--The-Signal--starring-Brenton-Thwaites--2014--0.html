Movie: The Signal, starring Brenton Thwaites (2014)20141024
This is a crazy movie and leaves a lot of neat questions open. I
think the various reveals are earned really well. I think the
biggest problem is that you really can't decide if his friends are
real and there's no inkling as to the purpose of him going to the
other world: what do the aliens want? I do like the way that he
figures out what is going on and that there are so many other
failed experiments. I think one unfortunate thing is that it
doesn't really explain what was going on with the cow or Jonah:
were they just misdirections or did they mean anything?
