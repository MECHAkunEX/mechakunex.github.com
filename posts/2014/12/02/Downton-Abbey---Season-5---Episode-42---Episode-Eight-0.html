Downton Abbey - Season 5 - Episode 42 - Episode Eight20141202
This was a nice conclusion to a lot of the plot lines, although
in retrospect, I feel like this was a very weak season without a
lot of spectacles. I like the Rose is married, although I wonder
if that means she's off the show because she won't live with
them. Similarly, Tom is leaving. I'm very surprised that Anna was
arrested; hopefully it won't be drawn out because otherwise it's
the same plot as what happened to Bates.
