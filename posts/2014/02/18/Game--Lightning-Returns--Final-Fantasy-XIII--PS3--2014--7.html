Game: Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy XIII (PS3, 2014) (8)20140218
I played for about six hours today. I spent the whole time doing
sidequests and killing all the enemies that popped up a long the
way. I've built a list of what side-quests I've done so I can be
on the look out for more and just have a few more to do. Most of
the remaining ones are related to the ultimate monsters. I read a
message board about the dragon and was really scared off. I was a
bit annoyed though because I tried to fight it, but it didn't
appear in the area. I exterminated two hard enemies---the Reaver
and the Protoflorian---which was pretty cool. I find that fighting
in the Wildlands is the best because Chocobodin helps you. The
Reaver's sword is amazing and it inspired me to change up my
schemata set up a lot. I am using the Ninja outfit a lot and
optimized around long combos with it. It seems to be performing
well. After doing the sidequests, my main goal will be to farm
Soul Seeds before the time to go to the final stages of the game.
