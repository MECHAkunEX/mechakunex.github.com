Game: Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy 13 (PS3, 2014) (2)20140212
I played today for about five hours and made it almost all the way
through Snow's plot-line. I really like exploring and only felt
like I wasted a little time waiting for appointments. When I
finished the Third Day, I thought it was weird that even though I
had done a ton of side quests, I didn't get any Yggdrasil
leaves. I suppose only main quest things really count? I don't
know.

I'm getting a lot better at the combat system and I've been
working on better strategies and better specializing my
schemata. I got some really cool weapons from seemingly special
events.

I like the dress-up part of the game a lot and wish that there
wasn't as big of a connection between the powers of an outfit and
what it looks like, because I would want Lightning to wear the
mauve dress all the time, except that I don't like its power set
up.
