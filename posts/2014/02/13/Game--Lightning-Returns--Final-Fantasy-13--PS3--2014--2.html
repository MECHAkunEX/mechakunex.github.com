Game: Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy 13 (PS3, 2014) (3)20140213
I played for about an hour and a half. I finished Snow's part and
went to the Wild Lands and started working on getting the
chocobo.

I had a cool moment: it was 11:30 and I wanted to get back to
Yusnaan to visit the diviners for noon, but I knew I wouldn't make
it and I had no EP. I fought a Reaver and got 2, so I teleported
and then used Chronostasis and got to the Diviners by 12:01. It
was awesome.

Unfortunately, when I was running through Luxorian, I found
Louise's dead body because I didn't do that quest to completion,
because I was just exploring that area on some downtime for
Yusnaan. I was really annoyed about this because it didn't say
that the quest was time-limited in the menu and I wouldn't have
started it if I knew that either. Bummer.
