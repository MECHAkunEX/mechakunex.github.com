Book: The Newman Resident, by Charles Swift (2014) (9)20140228
55: It's neat how quickly and believably Carol got on Richard's
side. The action at the school is pretty believable too. I assume
that Christopher will be in the basement. I wonder what goes on
there and what the purpose is. Perhaps the idea is that since the
Newman people don't really love the kids, they are able to justify
much stricter (and effective) punishment, but obedience isn't
actually what is good for kids, despite would often say to
themselves.
