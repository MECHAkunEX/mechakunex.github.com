Book: Ready Player One, by Ernest Cline (2011) (2)20140607
I love the way that he gets the first key. Classic!

--

I finished the book reading most of the day. This is a great "love
letter to the 80s". There are so many little choices moments. I
like the way things like Firefly and Whedonverse are mentioned. I
love that Halliday is "fascinated by D&D but doesn't have any
friends to play it with"---that's totally like me.

The whole ending sequence was really neat. I like how bizarre it is
and how the coin plays into it. I especially like the final moments
where Wade realizes that the OASIS isn't the most important thing.
