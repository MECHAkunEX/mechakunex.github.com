Movie: Pride & Prejudice, starring Kiera Knightley (2005) (2)20140323
After seeing the play the other day, we got a desire to watch this
movie. It was great. Something interesting though, is that it made
me like the play LESS because I feel like it was primarily based on
the scenes that the movie was based on to, which would make sense
because they are trying to adapt the same story, but it makes it
seem like the playwright didn't need to work as hard in deciding
what to show.
