Game: Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy 13 (PS3, 2014) (17)20140301
I played today for about three hours. I exterminated everything,
got the Ultima Weapon, and then beat the game. I beat the game on
my first try with a Magic guard. I used 3 X-Potions, 2 Phoenix
Downs, and 1 Arise. It was pretty intense.

I really liked the ending. It was very nice to remember the whole
game and I like the final moments. The extra scene at the end of
the credits was cool too.

I turned on the New Game+ on Hard for a little bit just to see how
things worked. I feel like I won't like doing every Side Quest
again because it is so "busy" but I will like fighting again. I
think the biggest thing I'm worried about is that it will take a
lot of time to get the materials to get the upgrade trophies. I
found that skipping the cut-scenes was a little annoying and slow.
