Book: Allegiant, by Veronica Roth (2013) (2)20140802
It's very interesting how serious of a romance this book
tells. Unlike Twilight, Tobias and Beatrice have such a complicated
relationship and have realistic arguments that are interesting to
pay attention to. I'm pretty surprised, actually.

It's really neat how long-term the project of the Bureau is. I wish
there were more explanation of what "genetic damage" is though. I'm
also very surprised that no outside forces in the government/etc
are opposed to the human experimentation.
