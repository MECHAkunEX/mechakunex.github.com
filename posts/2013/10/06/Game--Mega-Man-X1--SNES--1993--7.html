Game: Mega Man X1 (SNES, 1993) (8)20131006
I played through this today with my son watching in about three
hours. I got everything and beat everything up to Sigma's final
form. I tried it a bunch of times but eventually gave up with my
best effort just getting him down to about 60% with one more
sub-tank unused. I dislike the steep difficulty in the second form
of most Mega Man bosses. On this playthrough of the game, I was
quite annoyed by the control scheme and that you can't rearrange
buttons. My hands hurt a lot at certain moments from trying to
hold a charge shot while wall-jumping all over.
