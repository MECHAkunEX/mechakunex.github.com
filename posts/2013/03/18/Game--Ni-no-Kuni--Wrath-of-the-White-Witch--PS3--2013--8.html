Game: Ni no Kuni: Wrath of the White Witch (PS3, 2013) (9)20130318
I played today for two and a half hours and beat the game. I
basically just had to go through the final dungeon and beat the
final boss marathon. I didn't find the bosses that challenging.
The formula was to use the last two spells over and over and then
use items to get back some MP. It was a bit of a disappointing
ending. In addition, there's not really a final cut-scene... they
spent most of their "ending" juice on the faux-ending is seems.

Overall, I'd say that this game was beautiful and mechanically
sound, but too boring for my taste. There was a lot of variability
in the game... like the treat system and the huge number of
familiars... that I never really needed to use, which felt like a
let down. I wish there would have been more reason to switch
familiars, for example, rather than just find a few that I had
affinities for and use them the whole time. I also wish that
faster modes of ground transportation would have unlocked, because
moving around in the game started to feel really tedious.

I can imagine recommending the game to a kid, but I don't think I
would to an adult and definitely not a marginal RPG fan. If you
are just going to play one or two RPGs, there are much better
options.
