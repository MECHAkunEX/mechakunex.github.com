Game: Ninja Gaiden 3 (NES, 1991)20130319
Although I've beaten the other Ninja Gaiden games, I found this
one to be too much pain to be worth it. I got to 2-2D after 20
minutes and got sent all the way back to the beginning of 2-2A,
despite there being a lot of distance between those points and
many screen transitions. It just felt like I had done these games
too many times and I don't have the patience to deal with the
randomness and jumpiness again. It felt like the enemies did more
damage on average this time, but that there was less kick-back,
which is nice. I didn't keep going.
