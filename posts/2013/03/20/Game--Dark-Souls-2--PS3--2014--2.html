Game: Dark Souls 2 (PS3, 2014) (3)20130320
I played for about two hours and did a bunch of random things. I
tried out the fists, but they didn't seem to do enough damage,
even though they went really fast. I think it could work, but it's
a bit scary. I tried to fight the Cyclops at the beginning and
they were way too tough still. I explored a lot and got a shortcut
plus the Fire Longsword. I stupidly lost about 10k souls while
trying to fight the enemies above the Turtle Knight. But I got
past them and beat the Last Giant! Woot! It was really intense
because I couldn't get hit at all, since I was at half-health and
hollowed.

After that I started exploring more and died once against the
Turtle Knights because I was trying to look for a place to hide
from the bow using hollow. On my way back, I died again and lost
2.5k souls. I decided to book it to the bonfire and amazingly made
it, even though two Turtles followed me into the room and I had to
dodge around and kill them. It was amazing.

The game is really hard and I'm considering using armor, but still
not gaining levels, so I have a little bit of protection from
things like arrows. I haven't done it yet, but I'm thinking of it.
