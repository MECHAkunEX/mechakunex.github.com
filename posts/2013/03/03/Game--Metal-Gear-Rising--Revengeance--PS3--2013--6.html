Game: Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance (PS3, 2013) (7)20130303
I played for about two hours this morning and /only/ fought the
last boss over and over. It was totally brutal. Eventually, I had
a good run and got him to 72.7%, then the next time (30 minutes+
later) my best was 91.9, and then after another 30 minutes or so,
I beat him. Ouch.

I thought the final boss was ridiculously hard and only slightly
unfair, particularly once I realized that you didn't actually have
to slice up the blocks he threw at you and could just avoid them.

Overall, I thought the story was neat and felt very "Metal Gear",
which I like. I thought the gameplay was amazing I intend to
continue playing it and trying to master it. It's really fun and I
think it will be even more fun once I free myself up a bit with
upgrades, etc. I think the biggest gameplay flaw was the camera
not staying on the target you've chosen and it being painful to
select a particular target.
