Game: Breath of Fire (SNES, 1993) (6)20131117
I beat the game today in about three hours. Once you get Agni, the
game stops being challenging, because the form just destroys
everything. I fought most of the final battles on "Auto", only
stopping occasionally to use a Cure item. The ending of the game
is fun though. I think the other big problem with the game is that
the entire magic system is almost totally useless. I barely ever
cast attack spells with anyone and only with Bleu
occasionally. Similar, Nina only used her healing, reviving (just
a few times), and fortifying (just a few times) and never needed
to use the others. The game is definitely fun though... the plot
is simple but entertaining enough. The characters are memorable,
especially because of the "fable" quality of them being talking
animals.
