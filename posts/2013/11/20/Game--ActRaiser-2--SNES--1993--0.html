Game: ActRaiser 2 (SNES, 1993)20131120
I played the Ice Palace stage for about five Continues. It took a
little while to figure out how to double jump, but even once I
knew how to do it, it was still very difficult to control because
you glide so easily. This game is such a let down from
Actraiser 1. There's nothing really special about it and the
Overworld screen seems like a lame tack on and not something
interesting like the old system.
