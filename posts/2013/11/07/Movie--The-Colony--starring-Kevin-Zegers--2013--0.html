Movie: The Colony, starring Kevin Zegers (2013)20131107
This movie started off very cool and with a unique concept. Once
the bad guys appeared, it started to go down hill as it reveled in
gore a bit too much and lost its heady/intellectual base. I think
it really totally falls apart when they get back to their home
colony and it becomes a giant fight. They even make sure that the
cannibals never speak and were black masks, so that you dehumanize
them and treat them like zombies/animals.

The movie shows that it totally lost its soul in the final line
when Sam says that there is only one rule left: Survive. The
problem with this is that it goes against what the movie is about:
the fact that cannibalism is never okay and you shouldn't do it,
even though it could help you survive. Unless, of course, they
meant it in some deep way where Sam has had his evilness unlocked
by smashing off the head of the bad guy, but I doubt that.

After looking at the Wikipedia page, I was impressed by the super
small budget.
