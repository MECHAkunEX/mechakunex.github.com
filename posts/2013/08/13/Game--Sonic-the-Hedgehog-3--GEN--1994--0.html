Game: Sonic the Hedgehog 3 (GEN, 1994)20130813
I played the first two levels of this. It was basically the same
as the other Sonic games I've played. You can tell that there's a
lot of cute stuff in it to make it play better and with more
flavor. But, it's still not nice enough to make me want to play it
through.

I was amazed at how hard it was to look at the special stages. I'd
heard it was bad... but man... it really makes your eyes bleed.
