Game: Tales of Xillia (PS3, 2011)20130821
I played tonight for about three hours. It is pretty awesome. The
character dynamics are already fun after the first few scenes. The
battle system is really neat and actually a bit different than
Graces, but still clearly within the same framework. I had a bit
of a rough time at first, but once I realized that no one would
heal unless I did as Jude, then I rearranged the Strategy and
started taking control. It is still a bit frantic in fights, which
I enjoy. I'm excited to unlock higher difficulty levels though,
presuming you can do so.

I find the character progression system to be really
overwhelming. I can't tell if I should just do it auto-pilot or
meticulously look at every option and choose the just right ones.

I found Milla's voice actress to be either a weirdo or overdoing
it with the lisp.
