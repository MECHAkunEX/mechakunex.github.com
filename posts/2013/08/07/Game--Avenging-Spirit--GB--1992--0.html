Game: Avenging Spirit (GB, 1992)20130807
I played this for ten minutes and got to the third level. The
Kirby-like system of possessing enemies is very interesting and it
is clear that different enemies are better in different
situations. The platforming is really bad though. I feel like they
tried to compensate for the cramped screen with "floaty" jump
controls that give you much more leniency with where you will
land, but that this was an unwise choice and it is just
annoying. It has the annoying Ninja Gaiden-like enemy respawning
when you scroll their spawn points. Ouch. I can see how this game
is very interesting and worth thinking about, but it definitely
doesn't feel like a classic worth devoting a lot of attention to.
