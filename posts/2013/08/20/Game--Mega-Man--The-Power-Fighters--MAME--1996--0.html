Game: Mega Man: The Power Fighters (MAME, 1996)20130820
I credit-fed through this game in about twenty minutes. At first
glance, it is a bosses-only version of Mega Man, but with really
beautiful arcade graphics and a little bit weaker game-play, since
there's not as much to do in a small fighting game stage. The
bosses come from a lot of different games and are pretty fun to
fight. I'd say that the concept works in the game, but it's not
cool ENOUGH.
