Game: Popful Mail: Magical Fantasy Adventury (Sega CD, 1995)20130820
I played until after the first boss of this one. It felt like a
really competent Monster World or Wonder Boy game. A
side-scrolling, arcadey RPGy game. The voice acting seemed like it
thought it was all that, but was very meh. The control was pretty
tight, but didn't really have much variety. I thought it was just
okay.
