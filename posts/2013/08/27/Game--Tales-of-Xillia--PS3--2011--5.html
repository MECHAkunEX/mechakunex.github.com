Game: Tales of Xillia (PS3, 2011) (6)20130827
I played today for about three hours and got past the Jiao fight.

The new characters are interesting. It is especially strange how
many voice actors were also in Persona: Teepo sounds like Chie and
Elize sounds like Nanako, and then Jude is Teddy.

There was a really funny dialogue scene where Milla doesn't like
the picture of her on a Wanted poster and Jude has to reassure her
that she is pretty. It reminds me of the other funny instances
where she says that her servant (a guy) picked out her outfit and
that the Wind God, Slyph, does her hair.

I'm conflicted about Alvin. They're making him seem really
sketchy, but of course he has to be good eventually because you
spend so many resources on him. I wonder what will happen.

I though the fight with Jiao was /very/ hard. I use about 5 Life
bottles and had a lot of tough tactical moments, especially with
his monster summoning.

I think it is weird that the Lilium Orbs (the character
progression system) are a part of the character's world and that
they acknowledge their existence. I don't really understand what
they're supposed to mean to the characters.
