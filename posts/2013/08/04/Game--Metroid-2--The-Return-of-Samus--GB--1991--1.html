Game: Metroid 2: The Return of Samus (GB, 1991) (2)20130804
I played today for about three hours and got up the start of
Area 10. Area 9 was a real pain, because the Omega Metroids take
so many missiles and I ran out. I had to go back to Area 6 and get
a refresh... I wonder if I'll still make the 5 hours deadline? I'm
nervous about the ending area, because the Head-hugger Metroids
are always such a pain in Metroid games.

--

Later in the day I played for about thirty minutes and beat it. I
first waltzed to the Queen Metroid and was destroyed. The
Head-huggers weren't hard. But the Queen was. So I restarted and
used a Save state (my first in the game) outside the Queen's
chamber. I eventually beat it on my sixth or seventh try after
that using the Stomach technique. It was fun. My final time was
03:20, which means I spent about 1:09 on wasted runs.

I'd say that this is a good game, but not as amazing as other
ones.
