Game: Final Fantasy 7: Bootleg (hack) (Steam, 1997) (2)20131224
I played for about four hours this morning and got out of
Midgar. Something I failed to mention in the original post that is
kind of cool is that since I'm playing on PC, I can use a SNES
controller. The game only uses 2D movement, so it works really
well. Midgar is always memorable for me because I remember getting
out the first day I played and feeling that the game was going to
be huge.

I'm cheating a tiny bit because I'm using an AP boosting thing. I
find the materia development system in the game to take far too
long and require far too much grinding. So far it hasn't been game
breaking because I have impoverished MP and can't effectively use
the spells I unlock.

--

I got really sick after my first round of the game and took a long
nap. When I woke up, I played again for the rest of the night,
about five hours. I got to the middle of Cosmo Canyon. I had one
major set-back where I did at Dyne and didn't save after I ended
Corel Canyon. That was very brutal. I also had a hard time getting
Yuffie because of the green monsters that petrify you. I'm not
sure I will use her, but it is nice to have everyone in the game.
