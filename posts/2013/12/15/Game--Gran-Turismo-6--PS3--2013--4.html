Game: Gran Turismo 6 (PS3, 2013) (5)20131215
I played for a little more than an hour today. I beat two sets of
National B races. I decided to do the city races with my Focus ST
after doing the FF series with it too. It was much easier to
control and the opponents were scaled to it, which made it not too
hard.

--

Later in the day I did another 45 minutes and did the three 5
minute races with the Focus ST. They were pretty fun. It's nice to
get into the zone on a particular track and car then do it over
and over again.

If I keep at this pace (11% in 8 hours) then I will play for 75
hours, which sounds too low. It's also interesting to me how much
money I've made. It seems like a lot since I haven't had to buy
more than one car yet.

I recently watched a video of GT5. It's amazing how much faster
loading GT6 is.
