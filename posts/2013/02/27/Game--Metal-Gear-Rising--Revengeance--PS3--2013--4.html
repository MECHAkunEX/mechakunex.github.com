Game: Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance (PS3, 2013) (5)20130227
I played for about an hour today and made it through two stages
and the first fight of the next one. The two stages were very
short: Escape from Denver had four fights, I think, and the next
was just a boss fight. When I was doing Escape from Denver, I
suddenly realized it was part of the earlier Denver stage but
backwards so it looked different. I remember Kamiya saying they
did that with Bayonetta too.

The fight with Sam was pretty cool and the parrying technique
wasn't trivial to implement. In general, my parrying is so good
now that I can take on numerous enemies of a type where one would
have brought me down before.

It was cool to see Sunny.

I closed out more of the upgrade shop.
