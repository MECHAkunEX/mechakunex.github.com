Game: Spelunky (XBLA, 2012)20130209
I played this game for about an hour and a half. I got to the
second Jungle level once, but other than that would rarely get
past the second or third stage.

I like the concept of roguelikes and I like games that are
primarily skill based. I like the idea of this game a lot, but I
haven't got the point where I love it.

I think the running speed is a little too fast and I haven't
gotten used to it yet. I get the impression that the way to play
this game and succeed is to go incredibly slowly and calculate
every risk. At this point I feel like that won't be a very fun way
to play, because in platformers I love the feeling of running and
having flow.
