Game: Fez (XBLA, 2012) (3)20130209
I finished the game and did everything possible this morning. Some
of the final things were crazy. Changing the system clock to get
the clock to move was absurd. I got the tome and after looking
through it, just read the translation online. I don't really care
that much about the world of Fez, but it was cute. I translated a
sign that said: "Trapped in a Fez factory, please send help."
Turning the skulls was funny, because I could've done it so early
but didn't realize about grabbing things yet. I got four crashes
total in the game, I think. The 64-cube ending was neat and kind
of the opposite of the other one: zoom out vs zoom in.

Overall, I enjoyed the game. The puzzles were pleasant and the
weird deciphering stuff was unique. I ended up needing 3 pages of
notes and it was fun to make them.
