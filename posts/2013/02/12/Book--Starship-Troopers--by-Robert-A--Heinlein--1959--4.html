Book: Starship Troopers, by Robert A. Heinlein (1959) (5)20130212
I read through the part where he becomes an officer and it was
very fun. I thought the explanation of how the Federation started
and why it succeeds was fascinating. Similarly, there was a while
back when there was a cool discussion of why spanking is essential
for societal order.

The idea of most of the Navy being women and having shaved heads
is funny.

The long discussion of how many officers there are and how they
are organized is funny knowing the context of the author.

I finished the book today as well and was a bit amazed at how
anti-climactic it is from the perspective of the Bug War. From the
perspective of Johnnie's life, it's definitely a meaningful story
though.
