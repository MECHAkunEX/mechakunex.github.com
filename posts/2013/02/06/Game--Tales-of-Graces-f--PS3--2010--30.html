Game: Tales of Graces f (PS3, 2010) (31)20130206
I played in the afternoon and beat the game after two hours. The
total time to win was 6 hours and 12 minutes. Something amazing
happened, I had the various spheres on the mixer the entire time
and finally got 1 Darkened Ore in the last hour of gameplay and an
Enasphere in literally the walk up to where you fight the last
boss (so I had to turn around, save, and get it made into Asbel's
best sword.) I beat Lambda in 60 seconds, as well. Nice!

Next, I fought Lambda again on Chaos and used the Cheria piggyback
trick. The key was to get hurt by a ranged attack and beforehand
get rid of his Nova barrier right away with a quick spell.

After doing that, I played for another two hours-ish and got to
the Lastalia Core in the Future arc. In the evening I played for
another 30 minutes and beat the game... killing the Fodra Queen
in 60 seconds (actually 24) on the first try. Nice!

After doing the future arc, my grade is now 3551.

My next goal is the Platinum PSN trophy... the only barriers are
titles for the men and a 9999 mixer... which all seem pretty
painful to get. I'm going to look into the details tomorrow and
decide if I will do it. I'll also look up my battle stats for the
New Game+, I think that will be interesting.
