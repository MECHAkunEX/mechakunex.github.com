Game: Mark of the Ninja (XBLA, 2012) (2)20130219
I played for about three hours today. I did the Blade at His Neck
level twice, so I could go through with combat and without so I
could get all the stars (I got all the other things the first try
with the Stealth Suit, but missed a scroll and had to back-track a
lot, plus wasn't getting a good score.) I used Hunter the second
time because I knew it was a combat-heavy level. 

The next level, I played through twice, but not because I wanted a
different score, but because the game crashed in a Challenge Room,
which was a major bummer. When I did it the second time, I used
the Stealth Suit and didn't touch an enemy so I could get all the
bonuses. It was very hard to do the Seals, but very satisfying as
well.
