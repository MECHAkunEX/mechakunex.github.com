Game: Mark of the Ninja (XBLA, 2012) (3)20130220
I beat this game today after playing for about two and a half
hours. The last two levels were fun, although I find the forced
walking sequences excruciating. I didn't even replay the ending
to make the other choice because I didn't want to go through the
walking.

After I beat it, I did a few more levels to get achievements, but
I got annoyed pretty fast trying to find a level to do a few of
them on, so I didn't get two of them. I decided not to play New
Game Plus, right now at least, so I can start a new game. But I
might in the future.

Overall, I found this game to be pretty amazing. It definitely
goes in the canon for me.

I don't think it matters much, but my final score was 243,950 and
my rank was 1729/42718.
