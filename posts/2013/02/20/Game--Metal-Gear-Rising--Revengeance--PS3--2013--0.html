Game: Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance (PS3, 2013)20130220
I started this game tonight. I like that there is no pre-title
screen movie and it just gets right in to an awesome animation of
Raiden.

When I hit start, I decided to play on Hard... and I think it was
a good choice. I only played for about an hour and had three
little skirmishes and three boss fights. The boss fights were very
hard as I was still just learning the game; I think I did each at
least 5 or 6 times. Even the skirmishes feel hard because I
haven't really mastered the parrying system yet, but it is a lot
of fun.

This game is basically Metal Gear Bayonetta, which is
great. (Although parrying is a lot different than dodging and the
ninja run doesn't really have a corollary in Bayonetta.) I'm
super excited about continuing it.

From a story/style perspective, it totally feels like the Metal
Gear world. The characters, what they talk about, the feel, etc
are all right. They've even done a great job using the same fonts
and visual motifs from Metal Gear Solid 4. I like being in the
world and I like seeing the world using a different gameplay
format. It's neat.

Total time: 1:15
