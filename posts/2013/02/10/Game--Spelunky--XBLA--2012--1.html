Game: Spelunky (XBLA, 2012) (2)20130210
I played for about an hour today and switched to using my arcade
stick so that I know how to. My wife and I talked about trying the
local co-op, but I only have one normal Xbox controller, so she'll
have to use that. Playing with stick feels like it forces me to
move a bit slower and methodically, which is good. I think the
hardest thing to do is run which, again, is a good thing.

I got to the Jungle two times in my little play session and was
able to unlock the short-cut to it.  I read a bit of a FAQ about
the achievements and they are very intimidating. I feel like I
will just want to beat it normally. I'm not sure if I should try
to play through it from the start always or use the
short-cuts. The short-cuts are a nice way to feel like you're
making progress definitely, but it also seems like you are denying
yourself the ability to gather resources from the early stages.

I like using the Compass and bombs to get to the bottom quickly.

--

In the evening, I played for about 45 minutes, most of the time
with my wife. We found playing two player to be very
frustrating. The game requires a lot of coordination and it is
easy to make a mistake that totally disrupts the other
player---particularly because the camera follows the first
player. For example, if you accidentally fall or get shot, you
are likely to force scroll the screen and kill them. Not a recipe
for marital bliss.
