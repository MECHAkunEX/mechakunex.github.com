Game: Dust: An Elysian Tail (X360, 2012) (5)20130217
I played for about two and a half hours today. I got the reveal
about who Dust is and I think I am in the final dungeon. I got
some new abilities (double jump), so I went to go do some
exploration and expanded my map a lot. I feel like there will be
one more ability... something to do with Wind and I think I need a
Yellow Resonance Gem.

In the evening I played for another almost two hours and beat the
game. The ending was really epic. Overall, I enjoyed the game. I
feel like the combat has a pretty good flow, it's beautiful, and
there's a lot of neat Metroid-vania style exploration that's fun
to check off. I don't think there's a huge amount of depth to the
combat and I don't feel like going through and getting 100% on
everything, but it was definitely a good experience. I'm struck by
how much time I "lost" to playing on the Hardcore difficulty for
so long. I feel like it wasn't the kind of difficulty I like
though, because there's very little that you can do differently,
or at least it felt that way.

I'm very impressed that it was all made by one guy. Bravo!
