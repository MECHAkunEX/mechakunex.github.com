Game: Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance (PS3, 2013) (2)20130222
I played for another two hours tonight and made a bunch of
progress on the first mission, fighting a boss and going through
a fun chase sequence. The boss really forced me to learn the
parrying system, although I wasted a lot of health regeneration
items during the fight. I want to try again.

I haven't yet looked into whether I can replay story chapters to
get better scores or things that I missed. For instance, I noticed
what looked like an enemy intel station on top of roof but
couldn't figure out how to get to it. I assumed that I couldn't
but then when I got the next one is was for VR Mission 003, rather
than 002. In retrospect, I bet I could have used the Ninja Run to
get up.

I'm trying to take my time with the game and listen to every
codec conversation and get all the stuff. I think I'll play
through once and then go back and make a list of the stuff I
missed and get them.

I'm really enjoying the gameplay and just starting to master
it. I'm enjoying the world a lot too. I feel like there are little
things fans... like how Raiden's boss say "Keep you waiting, eh?"
and how one of the cyborg is named Sam, like in Vanquish.
