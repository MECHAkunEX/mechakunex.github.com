Game: Bangai-O HD: Missile Fury (X360, 2011) (10)20130104
I played for about twenty minutes and beat stage 42.

Stage 42 --- Eventually I got into a good grove with the Longai-O
and could beat him trivially. I hope that the experience will make
me better against them in other levels too. I had a few tough
experiences with the big ships kind of in the middle, but learned
to take one out then stay behind the other. The final room felt
very challenging, but really only took me three times. The key was
to get rid of the hedgehogs and then take it slow getting the
perfect angle to shoot the targets.
