Game: Hot Shots Golf: World Invitational (Vita, 2012) (9)20130110
I decided to take this off my list because I haven't played it in
so long and when I think about playing it, it makes me mad
because it's so hard to know what a good strategy would be.

Part of me feels like the game requires you to understand
something about golf... because it doesn't give you any assistance
understanding what will happen because of the wind, ground
curvature, or grass length, etc---but requires you to figure it
out. I don't find that my intuition for that stuff is growing.

Nevertheless, I definitely have fond memories of playing the game
and I think that it is very well made, so I think deserves a high
ranking.
