Movie: The Grey, starring Liam Neeson (2012)20130116
This was a very stirring movie. Neeson's character is so tough and
it's so tragic how some of the guys die. The final moments are
really powerful: man's determination to live at all costs and
despite everything. Even though I am a very faithful man, I'm glad
that they didn't have a cliche scene that would save Ottway and
give him faith in God. I think it is better that he did it himself.

The movie had way too much swearing, but I think the characters
would have acted liked that, so it reflects real-life in that
sense.
