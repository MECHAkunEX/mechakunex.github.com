Movie: Apocalypto, directed by Mel Gibson (2006)20130116
This was a really interesting movie. It's is practically without
dialogue for huge stretches and its setting is virtually without
compare. It feels incredibly realistic, although I don't really
have any way to verify that. I'm kind of amazed that it was made.

I think the most crazy part was when the lady is nearly drowning in
the pit and happens to give birth because the fall "shocked" her
into it. It was a little bit too crazy, but it worked in the story.

The movie was far more gory than it needed to be, but I think I
understand why Gibson designed it that way.

It's cool that at the core it is a "chase" movie.
