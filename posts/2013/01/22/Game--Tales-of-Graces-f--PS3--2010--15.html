Game: Tales of Graces f (PS3, 2010) (16)20130122
In the afternoon, I played for about two hours and in the evening
I played for about three. I spent the whole time unlocking places
in the Shuttle and doing side quests. A lot of the side quests
don't seem to be as meaningful as I was hoping, but the story
tidbits are interesting. I enjoyed the puzzles in the Sandshroud
Ruins; I enjoyed the play; I enjoy Pascal's ridiculous groping
animation whenever possible; Katz Korner is crazy; etc. Some of
the story bits are harder to appreciate because they have no
voice, which is only odd because the rest of the game does. I
think I'm getting towards the end of the side quests, and thus
I'll go to the end of the game soon.
