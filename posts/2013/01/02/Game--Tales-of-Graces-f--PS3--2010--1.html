Game: Tales of Graces f (PS3, 2010) (2)20130102
I played for about two hours this morning and got to the capital
with Richard. The story is very cute and I like the way it seems
to be foreshadowing what will happen when the kids are older. I
like the idea of seeing the characters at multiple stages of life.

----

Later in the day I played for another two-ish hours. I got to a
lot of tragedy in Asbel's life. I think they really sold his
change of personality and it is interesting too. I'm curious to
see what will happen. It's a little strange for him to go from one
trope to another though... first he's annoying brash anime
character and now he's annoying somber anime character.

The battles after he grows up are a lot more difficult and
interesting, too.

Total time: 5:00
