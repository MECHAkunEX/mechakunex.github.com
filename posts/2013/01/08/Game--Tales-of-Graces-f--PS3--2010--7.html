Game: Tales of Graces f (PS3, 2010) (8)20130108
This afternoon I played for about three hours. I got into the
tempering sub-system and it seems like it will be very
significant, but, of course, it means I have much fewer resources
for buying and building other things.

I got to the Rockgagong and thought the dialogue in there was
awesome. Being on the Evil difficult got really brutal once I was
inside too and I needed to switch my strategy around to have
Cheria and Sophie constantly healing.

--

Later in the day, I played for another hour or so and finished
the Rockgagong dungeon. The boss was very tough on Evil,
particularly when there were lots of parasites. I think I used 4
Life Bottles in a row when they first appeared before I thinned
the crowd a bit.

Exploring Yu *REDACTED*erte was fun and the conversation with the
president was neat. But, I think the voice actor doesn't sound
the part at all though.
