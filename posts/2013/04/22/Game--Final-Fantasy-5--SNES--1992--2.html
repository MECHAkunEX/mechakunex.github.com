Game: Final Fantasy 5 (SNES, 1992) (3)20130422
I played for about two and half hours today. The first half was
wandering around in the new world until I ran into the forest
where you meet the moogles. Moogles are so cute and I'm glad they
were introduced in this game... yet another thing that FF5 started
and shares with FF6. I love their song.

Next, I got to Galuf's castle and spent a lot of time grinding in
the basement. I'm closing to getting my characters to the point of
being Freelancers. Well, about half-way. I think I'm done grinding
jobs now because I have a good repertoire that will last for a
long time.

Also, I'm about to leave on a trip, so I'll be away from my SNES
for a while. When I get back, I doubt I'll want to spend the time
grinding... although maybe a little to get back into the feel of
the game.
