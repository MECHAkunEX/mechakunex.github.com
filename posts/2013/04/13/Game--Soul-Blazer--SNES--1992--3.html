Game: Soul Blazer (SNES, 1992) (4)20130413
I played today for about three hours and beat the game. I found
the game to continue to be very adorable. The final area wasn't
particularly hard, but it was very fun to get the Phoenix shot
upgrade.

Overall, I found the game to be very fun and cute. I would
definitely recommend it for the good mechanics and the nice
story. The story and mechanics, however, don't feel good enough to
me to warrant many replays, so I don't think I will play it
again. But I definitely will think of it fondly.

I'm excited to see what connections future Quintet games will have
to this world.
