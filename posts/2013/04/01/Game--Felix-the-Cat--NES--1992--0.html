Game: Felix the Cat (NES, 1992)20130401
I played this for about fifteen minutes and got to 2-3. The game
is a totally boring and normal platforming game. I found the
controls and hit detection to be quite slippery. It felt too easy
to slide into an enemy. The sprite size seems a bit wrong too,
because it's easy to scroll off the screen and then come back to
something that kills you, which feels very un-fair and
un-fun. The best part of the game were the adorable power-ups and
how they stacked atop one another. That was neat.
