Game: Summer Carnival '92: Recca (NES, 1992)20130401
I played this game for a bit and did each of the modes. I had
tried it once before. It is really crazy and amazing that it is
an NES game. The technology, and the screen size in particular,
really make it hard to do a game like this well. I love shmups
and bullet hell ones in particular, but this game was at the
wrong time and place to really do it well, I think.

So... my verdict is: visually impressive for the time, but not
playable today.
