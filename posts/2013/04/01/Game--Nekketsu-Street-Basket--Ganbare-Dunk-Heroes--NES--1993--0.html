Game: Nekketsu Street Basket: Ganbare Dunk Heroes (NES, 1993)20130401
This game takes the insanity of the Kunio sports games to a new
level. It's a ridiculous take on basketball---you are literally
fighting while "playing basketball" and then there are three hoops
are different heights. I didn't really understand what to do and
especially not how to get the ball to the higher baskets. I
basically just played "defense" by hurting all the enemies while
Riki got some baskets. It was a very frenetic and crazy game... I
can't really say I enjoyed it, because it felt so out of control,
but I feel like I could appreciate it a bit.
