Game: Nekketsu Kakutō Densetsu (NES, 1992)20130401
This game has a great concept: a fighting game in the River City
Ransom engine. The game feels pretty good, but gets old pretty
quickly. I can imagine having a lot of fun with it with friends,
especially when I was young. The style of the game feels a lot
like Smash Brothers, in particular with the four independent
players. I can imagine playing this A LOT with a group of
friends. It is especially cool that your progress is saved in a
password, so you could "bring" your character to a friend's
house. But, ultimately, for me today it doesn't have enough
appeal.
