Game: F-Zero (SNES, 1990)20130405
This was an interesting game that felt like a window into its
age, but it wasn't really fun today given how much racing games
have come. I found the graphics to be a little hard to look at it
because of the hypnotic patterns. I found the handling to be
really strange too. I got Rank 1 on the first two courses and
then crashed out, and dropped out.
