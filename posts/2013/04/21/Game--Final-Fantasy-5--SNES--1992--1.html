Game: Final Fantasy 5 (SNES, 1992) (2)20130421
I played for most of this morning and go to the first town of
Galuf's world. I still haven't really ventured outside the first
few jobs, because I'm focusing on mastering them. Most of the jobs
that seem exciting, like Dragoon, aren't really that good when you
read the details about them.

Some of the bosses in this game are really finicky with the random
number generator. For example, the boss of the Ancient *REDACTED*rary
creamed me the first four times, then when I prepared a new
strategy, he never even attacked! He just used status effects and
didn't make use of them.

Something annoying about this game (and almost all other RPGs)
after playing Mystic Quest is that you can't jump over people who
are in your way!

When I learned about Butz' history, it was a little to awkward
for his dad to yell, "Stella!"

Finally, after getting the airship, I found it very confusing what
I was supposed to do... I just wandered around trying to find new
places to go... eventually I accidentally discovered you had to go
/back/ to the ruins.

I think next time I play I'll have to grind a little, because I'm
finding the random battles taking too much out of me.
