Game: Final Fantasy Mystic Quest (SNES, 1992) (5)20130418
I played today for about four hours and beat the game. It was a
lot of fun. The last area is neat because there's a bit of a boss
rush. The final boss is pretty easy because Cure devastates
him. One bad thing near the end was a tedious fetch quest across
the world with no fast travel. At least their weren't random
battles to get in the way.

Overall, I think this is a very fun game. It's short and concise,
yea, but it does what it wants to and does it well.

In the credits, I noticed "T. Nomura"... is that Tetsuya?
