Game: Axelay (SNES, 1992)20130414
I played through this game in about an hour, using an infinite
lives cheat code. I had the standard complaints about the
invisible bullets and horizontal-ness that I normally have about
old shmups. BUT, the visuals and enemies in this game were so
amazing that it was definitely worth playing through to see them
all. The two kind of levels was a really neat touch that I
enjoyed.
