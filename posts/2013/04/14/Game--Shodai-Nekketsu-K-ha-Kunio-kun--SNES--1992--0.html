Game: Shodai Nekketsu Kōha Kunio-kun (SNES, 1992)20130414
I played this game for a bit, but I got stuck at the entrance to
the hotel and couldn't get out. I could understand enough of the
Japanese to read the menus and some of the dialogue like "Kunio
went up in level!" but I didn't understand what I was supposed to
do. The game is a lot slower paced in the combat than River City
Ransom, so it wasn't as exciting. I read online that they dropped
the shopping part and it is more like an open-world beat-em up
with RPG-style levels, rather than choives in what you learn,
etc. It doesn't sound as exciting.
