Game: Joe & Mac (SNES, 1991)20130410
I played this for the first two levels. I remember seeing this
game in Nintendo Power and being incredibly impressed by the
visuals. Now that I am older and playing it, it feels like the
visuals are the main reason for this game being terrible. The
sprites are too big for the action and the hit boxes are real
bad. Amazingly, it is a 2D platformer with camera problems. When
you die, if you move to the right, then the screen doesn't scroll
with your ghost. This made it so I couldn't see the next platform,
I had to go backwards then right again to get it to
scroll. There's really nothing special about this game at all.
