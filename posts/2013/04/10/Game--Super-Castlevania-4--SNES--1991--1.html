Game: Super Castlevania 4 (SNES, 1991) (2)20130410
I played this for about thirty minutes. I got to 3-3 on my first
continue. 

This game is a lot worse than the NES Castlevania games and it is
nowhere close to Dracula X. I think the biggest problem is the
really stiff transitions between states. You can very clear see
the game stutter when you jump, start swinging, get on a ladder,
etc. Unlike the first game, where the deliberateness of the game
seems to be a purposeful feature... in this game it seems like a
bad mistake.

In addition, I agree with the Sequelitis estimation of the
negative aspects of the eight-way whip: the combat sequences are
simply easy. I barely got hit a single time and the only times I
died, it was because of falling during platforming (primarily
during swinging segments.)
