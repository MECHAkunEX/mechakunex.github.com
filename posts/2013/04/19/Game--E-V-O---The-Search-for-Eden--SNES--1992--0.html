Game: E.V.O---The Search for Eden (SNES, 1992)20130419
I played this game for about forty minutes. I couldn't get pass
the first boss and I grinded for a bit so I could get enough
evolution points to get a horn, thinking it would stack with my
jaws, and then I lost it... then I didn't know how to attack with
the horn. What a bummer.

The concept of this game is really interesting and cool, but the
implementation is pretty bad. The details of fighting are
terrible. Enemies just go near you and you get hit and can get
hit-stun-locked very easily. The exactness of each bite you need
to land is pretty rough too. I think the worst part of the
game... at least what I played... was the ridiculous amount of
grinding you needed to do to get anything. Platformers with
grinding like this are the worst... particularly when it isn't fun
to fight.
