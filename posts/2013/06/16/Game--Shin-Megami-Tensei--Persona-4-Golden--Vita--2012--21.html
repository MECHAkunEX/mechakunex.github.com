Game: Shin Megami Tensei: Persona 4 Golden (Vita, 2012) (22)20130616
I played for a long time today and beat the main boss. There's
still a lot more social stuff in the game and I got to February
2nd. I think there's one last dungeon, because some of the allies'
Personas got stronger. But, I won't be able to play for about a
week. I'll have to finish it then.

I was thinking today about how different the meta-games of Final
Fantasy and Persona are. In Final Fantasy, you basically never use
magic power except against bosses, because it is too expensive to
waste on basic enemies. In Persona, because of the weakness and
strength systems, you are forced to use magic because otherwise
the fights are very hard. This then depletes your magic power,
forcing you to either pay a lot of money or leave the
dungeon... thus losing the opportunity to have another afternoon
with your social links, since you have to go back. It's pretty
ingenious how a little thing in the battle system connects to the
social system at the meta-level.

Additionally, it is interesting in Persona that the Main *REDACTED*acter
can never die in battle or you lose, as opposed to many other
games that allow the main person to die.
