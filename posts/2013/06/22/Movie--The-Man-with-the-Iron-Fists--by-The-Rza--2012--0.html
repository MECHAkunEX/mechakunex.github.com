Movie: The Man with the Iron Fists, by The Rza (2012)20130622
I really like these Chinese fantasy movies with mystical fighting
techniques, etc---especially Bronze Body and the Gemini Stance. I
thought The Rza was cool and the rap music was great in the fight
sequences. The implied racy scenes with Russell Crowe were weird
and I'm not sure what the point was, other than gawking. There was
a lot of similarity between this and Kill Bill, which is awesome
too, especially with the cool cuts. Near the end of the movie, I
greatly enjoyed Crowe's character's rhymes and puns.
