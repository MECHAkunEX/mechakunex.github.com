Movie: Star Trek XI: Star Trek (2009)20130721
The beginning of this movie makes me cry every time. I've seen it
three times and it always does it.

It's cool that there are lots of little ideas and quotes that come
from the other movies.

Scenes like when Scotty is stuck in the tubes are fun and show off
the ship and future technology, but they are ultimately
pointless. It's strange that they're there at all.

This is an awesome movie.
