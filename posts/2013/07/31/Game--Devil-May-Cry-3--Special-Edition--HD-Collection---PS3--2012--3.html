Game: Devil May Cry 3: Special Edition (HD Collection) (PS3, 2012) (4)20130731
I played today for about two hours and got to Mission 15. It was
very hard to fight Vergil. The Horse was pretty easy
though. Mission 14 was very confusing because it wasn't clear
where you were supposed to be going.

--

Later in the day I played for another two hours and got to Mission
19 (the one after the boss rush.) I think that means I'm basically
done because there's supposed to be 20 levels.

I found the boss fight against the Lady to be really annoying
because she moves around so much, but also not hard because I was
able to just use the Trickster teleport to close in on her very
fast.

I liked the Doppleganger fight a lot. It was cool to use something
in the environment to make them vulnerable and try to goad them to
go near it.

Total time: 10:00
