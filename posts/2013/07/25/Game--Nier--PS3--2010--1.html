Game: Nier (PS3, 2010) (2)20130725
I played today for about three hours. I left the Junk Heap and
went back to The Aerie for the boss fight there. It was pretty
cool, especially the 2D segment. Next, I went to the Facade, which
was really weird, and then The Barren Temple, which had a really
cool boss fight and the puzzle rooms were neat too. The Forest of
Myth was insane... I can't believe it drops you into a text
adventure. Insanity. The Haunted Manor felt like Resident Evil
with the fixed camera angles and gathering shaped keys... so
weird. The boss fight was pretty boring. I feel like I'm not at
the end of the first half because I've gathered the Sealed Verses.
