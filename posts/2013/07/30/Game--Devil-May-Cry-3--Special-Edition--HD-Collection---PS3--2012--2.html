Game: Devil May Cry 3: Special Edition (HD Collection) (PS3, 2012) (3)20130730
I played a lot today, like four hours, and got up to Mission 12. I
found all the boss fights to be very hard. I almost felt like
quitting fighting the Lightning Witch and the Light Demon they
were so hard. But, I persevered and discovered some new
techniques. This game feels like you have to be very lucky with
the moves and incredibly quick with your dash and movement. There
just isn't enough time to use normal movement and there's no
built-in "dodge" system like Witch Time, etc. It's intense and
enjoyable... although it doesn't feel as good as Bayonetta :)

Total time: 6:10
