Game: Shin Megami Tensei: Persona 4 Golden (Vita, 2012) (24)20130712
I played for about two hours today and finished the game. After
getting through Marie's dungeon, I went through right to the
end. Stupidly, I forgot to save my game and I didn't go to the
True Ending. Now, if I want to get to it, I will have to play
through the last 3 hours again! Ugh. I'm so mad at myself/the game
for not saving before the end. I even thought about it because I
was worried about not seeing everyone on the final day. One option
is to start up again but skip Marie's dungeon and just go through,
but that seems really lame too.

One thing that I enjoyed in the end was that one Valentine's Day
they really made you feel terrible for being with a lot of
different girls. I wonder what that would have been liked if I
wasn't on the "Lovers" path with them?

Overall, I enjoyed this game and will definitely play it again,
but I'm really mad at it right now and have other things on my
plate before giving it another go. Maybe in a week or so.
