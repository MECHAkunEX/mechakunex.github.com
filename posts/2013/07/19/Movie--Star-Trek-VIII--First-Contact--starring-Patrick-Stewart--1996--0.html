Movie: Star Trek VIII: First Contact, starring Patrick Stewart (1996)20130719
This movie has a really bizarre set up. I wish there was a better
introduction to the Borg.

It was very interesting to see the preamble to the "First Contact"
event in Star Trek history. I really like the way the guy isn't
interested in being a hero. It's a very intriguing idea to think
about knowing about how you will be remember and how you would
react to that knowledge.

The zero-G fight was really stressful if you really thought about
what was going on, but it was shot in such a passive style that it
seemed like it didn't matter at all.

It's so weird that Borg hardly react to the crew at all. I guess
they aren't supposed to be like "cells" of a larger creature, but
rather things that are semi-autonomous but get occasional commands.

I'm surprised when the Enterprise crew plan to blow up the ship,
they don't discuss how they will get back to the future. Although
they did talk about how the people still on Earth should stay there
and "not affect" history. But then the people on the Enterprise
looked like they were going to Earth... it seems like they *have*
to recover the Enterprise and then go home safely.
