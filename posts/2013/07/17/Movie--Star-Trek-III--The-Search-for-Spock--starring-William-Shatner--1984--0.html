Movie: Star Trek III: The Search for Spock, starring William Shatner (1984)20130717
The beginning of this movie was very fun as they were taking over
the ship. It was a little bit odd though because no one is
particularly attractive or spry. (They're all old men.) The acting
is so slow even when the scene is supposed to be tense and action
packed. It's very weird.

It's too bad that Saavik wasn't the same actress.

You can clearly tell that the movie was designed to be structurally
the same as the last movie with the Klingon leader playing the part
of Khan.

It was intense that they destroyed the Enterprise. 

The ending was a bit odd for me given that I don't have a huge
attachment to the series.
