Book: Scott Pilgrim, Vol. 3: Scott Pilgrim & the Infinite Sadness, by Bryan Lee O'Malley (2006)20100815
<p>So Volume 3 is pretty amazing too. I love all the Vegan
jokes. The relationship awkwardness is pretty brilliant too. It's
amazing that this whole volume is basically just two days, although
there were a lot of flashbacks, etc. I liked the subtle commentary
on hipsterism culminating in the girl who, when asked if she likes
the band, says "I'm not sure, I'll have to go home and think about
it." Nice. I can't wait to see the movie if they do it well.</p>
