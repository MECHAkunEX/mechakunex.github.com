Game: Axiom Verge (Steam, 2015) (4)20160107
I played for a few hours and fought a few bosses and got a few
things. I believe that I have all the upgrades now and it's just a
matter of doing some exploring before finding out where the final
boss is. The game is very clever with its new upgrade ideas. I
really liked this one moment where I grappled up and then
teleported through the platform I was underneath. I smiled because
I previously thought it was not possible to get through. I had
clearly been thinking too much in terms of other Metroid-style
games I'm familiar with. The story is also crazy.
