Game: Mercenary Kings (Steam, 2014)20160114
I played this for about two hours. I tried to use the SNES
controller but it didn't work and didn't let me rebind. I was very
annoyed about that, but I used the Steam controller. I think I am
going to go back and build a Joytokey config for it and try it out
because it seems like it should work. I found the moment-to-moment
play to be really awesome and the loop to be awesome. It kind of
sucks that I can go on a mission, do it successful, but then come
out and have nothing to upgrade on my character. I don't mind
grinding if there is always some progress made. I also wish I
could buy materials, because I feel like I have a surplus of
money. I played with a few people online and it was really fun,
although quite a bit easier than being alone. Although that really
made me feel like it was too hard by myself.

I like it so far and will keep playing.
