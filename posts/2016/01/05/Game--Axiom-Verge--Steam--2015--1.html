Game: Axiom Verge (Steam, 2015) (2)20160105
I played for a while and beat two bosses and got the next few
areas. The game is very fun and I agree with the reviews I've read
that the powers are very surprising and clever. It doesn't fall
into the same power tropes as other games in this genre, which is
refreshing. The bosses have all been fun and although daunting at
at first, I've been able to beat them basically without getting
hurt on the second try (or even the first, in one case!)
