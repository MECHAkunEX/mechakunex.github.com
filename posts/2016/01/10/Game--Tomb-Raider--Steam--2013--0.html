Game: Tomb Raider (Steam, 2013)20160110
This had a very strong and fun opening. My kids were watching
behind me and thought it was very scary. I had to turn it off once
they started using naughty language. I am always disappointed when
there's bad language in a game like this. I like Lara's voice
actress a lot though. I'll either switch to a different language
(if I can) or just play when they aren't around. I felt a little
dizzy after playing for a while, so I moved my seat further
back. I think that helped. I got up to the first tomb to
explore. The game does a really good job of making me want to
explore and find everything. There was this one QTE at the
beginning of the Mountain Temple area that I found very difficult
to do. I wonder if it is because I'm using the Steam controller.
