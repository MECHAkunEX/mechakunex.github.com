Movie: The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift, starring Lucas Black (2006)20160115
The opening of this movie is insane. It's sad that the kid from
Home Improvement is in it. It's ridiculous that the girl would
offer herself as the prize. The "fish out of water" nature of the
plot is kind of interesting, except that everyone he hangs out with
is American. I think that there's just enough Japanese-ness to be
confusing... for instance, there's no explanation made of what it
means to work in a hostess bar (what the girl's mom does.) I feel
like the movie is not as bad as it is often said to be. I really
liked the way that Han's character felt consistent with his
portrayal in the other movies. Overall, I'd say that the race
scenes are actually better and more nerving than most of the
mainline movies' race scenes.
