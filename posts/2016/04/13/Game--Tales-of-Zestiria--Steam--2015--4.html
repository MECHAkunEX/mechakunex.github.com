Game: Tales of Zestiria (Steam, 2015) (5)20160413
I played today for quite a while. I made it into the town across
the river and had a little bit of action there. Ultimately, it was
pretty boring. I feel like this game is just "wander to star,
watch anime scene, press the B button a lot." In contrast, Graces
f had a very complicated and detailed combat system. In this game,
even on the hardest difficulty, there's basically just two kinds
of battles: one where you press B and one where anything you do
seems to not work. It is very frustrating. I feel like it is a
waste of my time because I'm not in love with the characters. At
this point, the game has cost me $6.36 per hour and I don't really
enjoy it. Should I keep playing it even though I don't love it
just to "get my money's worth"? I feel like I'm going to drop it
and pay less attention to Tales for a while.
