Game: Dark Souls 3 (Steam, 2016)20160416
I started playing this with the Steam Controller but couldn't even
get past any of the menus. I think it registered it as a keyboard
& mouse so everything was totally messed up. I learned about how
to use my DualShock 4 controller and went with that. It feels
really nice. It took me almost 15 minutes to actually get started,
which was quite annoying.

I started as a female Depraved and had a real tough time on the
first boss, I think I fought it about ten times before beating
it. As usual, it felt awesome to overcome the challenge.

I made it to the Firelink Shrine and spent a lot of time wandering
around looking for the next place to go. I assumed I had to make
it through the guy with the katana, but had real trouble. I looked
online and learned that I had to teleport to a new place. D'oh!

I explored that place a little bit and saved up for a White
Soapstone. I spent quite a while helping other people and getting
to know what the area looked liked. It is a fun way to grind and
explore without being in a lot of real danger.

This game is pretty awesome!
