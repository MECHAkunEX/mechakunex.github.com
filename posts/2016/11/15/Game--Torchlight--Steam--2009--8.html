Game: Torchlight (Steam, 2009) (9)20161115
I finished the game today. There was a huge difficulty spike in
the last few levels. It was absurd how much easier the bosses were
than the enemies in their levels. The very last boss was
completely ridiculous in terms of HP. The whole end-game was
basically entirely meta-game, rather than the the actual
challenge of gameplay. I found that I was running out of gold to
buy healing potions really bad at the end too.

Overall, I enjoyed the game, but it definitely had a bad ending
and got too samey towards the end. I think I was hamstrung by
playing as the missile character because I couldn't survive
attacks very well. Next time I play a game like this, I will go
with the melee character.

This is basically the first time I've played a Diablo-like and I
definitely enjoyed it. In a while, I'll do the sequel, which I
own, and then maybe do Diablo 3.
