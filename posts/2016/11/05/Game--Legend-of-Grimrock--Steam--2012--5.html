Game: Legend of Grimrock (Steam, 2012) (6)20161105
I've decided to stop playing the game. I feel like there aren't
enough interesting choices in the game. I had to decide at the
beginning which kind of character I will want, but every time I
level up it is obvious what I should do any ultimately the
characters won't play that much different. The levels are entirely
linear, which would be fine if there was a fun gameplay challenge
to conquer them. However, I feel like the combat is very rote,
except when it is annoying that I have to re-key spells or that I
can't use keyboard commands to launch attacks. The puzzles are all
vaguely fun, but not unique enough. The ones that are the most
unique have been the most annoyingly trial-and-error like the
platform puzzle on the last floor. I played through half of the
game and I feel like I know myself enough to know that the rest of
the game will not be enjoyable enough. Sad.
