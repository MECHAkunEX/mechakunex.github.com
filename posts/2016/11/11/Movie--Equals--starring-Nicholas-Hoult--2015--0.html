Movie: Equals, starring Nicholas Hoult (2015)20161111
I thought that this would be a movie that was about the dystopia
and the way that the heroes escaped and so on. It is actually a
metaphor about teenagers falling in love for the first time in a
world that they perceive as being insensitive and antagonistic to
their emotions. On that level, it was very interesting, but it took
me almost until the end of the movie to realize what it was really
about. The details of the culture and how it became the way it is
were all very interesting and intriguing, but the movie followed
the typical pattern of not dwelling on them too much.
