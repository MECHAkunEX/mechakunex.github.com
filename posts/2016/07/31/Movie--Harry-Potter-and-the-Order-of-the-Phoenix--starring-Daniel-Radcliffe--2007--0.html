Movie: Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, starring Daniel Radcliffe (2007)20160731
We finished this audiobook yesterday and so it was time to watch
the movie. I feel like this is a case where the movie lacked so
much detail from the book, it was almost hard to understand. There
were definitely some parts that were much better in the movie, like
the torment of Umbridge and the actual meetings of the D.A., but
many other things were much more sketchy and left out.
