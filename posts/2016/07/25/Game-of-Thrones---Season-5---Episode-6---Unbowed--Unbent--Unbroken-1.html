Game of Thrones - Season 5 - Episode 6 - Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken (2)20160725
It is very interesting and cool how Arya is learning to play "the
game of faces". I'm not totally sure what she is learning here
and I wish it would move faster. I love the little connections in
the social network of the characters, such as Tyrion bringing
news of Jorah's father. Jaime's escapade seems pretty
pointless. Baelish's scheme with Cersei is so sneaky. I'm very
surprised that the Tyrells are both now in jail. Ramsay is so
terrible. I like that Cersei really is just mindlessly scratching
the paper, because she drops the pen at the end.
