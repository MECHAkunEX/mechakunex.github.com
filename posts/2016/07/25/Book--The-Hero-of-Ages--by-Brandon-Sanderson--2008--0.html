Book: The Hero of Ages, by Brandon Sanderson (2008)20160725
I started reading this today. The opening scenes were very cool. I
like that we have an explanation of sorts of why Marsh turned
against Sazed. I really don't like that Elend is a particularly
powerful Allomancer, because I really like Vin being the most
powerful one around. I really would have wanted her to be even more
powerful as a result of her touch with the Well. Nevertheless, it
was an awesome scene.

--

There are some really cool ideas in these opening chapters. I love
the journal entries at the top of each chapter: the idea that the
Lord Ruler really was more of a hero than people thought and that
he literally moved around the planet as a way to fight the
Mists... it's very intriguing. I like the idea that you can only
trust your thoughts because Ruin is so powerful. I suspect that the
Inquisitors killed all the Terrisman Keepers because it is a vital
ingredient in making them more powerful. This explains why the Lord
Ruler hunted them for so long.
