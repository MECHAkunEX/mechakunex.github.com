Game of Thrones - Season 3 - Episode 7 - The Bear and the Maiden Fair (2)20160708
I enjoyed the conversation that Jon had with Ygritte. It was very
cute to hear them toying with each other. It's interesting to
think about the past times that the Free-Folk have failed to
invade. Jon doesn't understand Orell's comments because he, as a
Stark, was raised to "die for honor" rather than "lie for
himself", so the idea of turning on people doesn't occur to him.

Dany's conversation and proceedings with the representative of
Astapor was great. I especially like when she fed the dragons.

Tyrion's conversation with Bronn was great.

Tywin had a great moment controlling Joffrey, who is totally
terrible. I liked how he explained about the dragon skulls.

Jaime's attempts to protect Brienne are cute. The visuals of the
bear fighting her were awesome.
