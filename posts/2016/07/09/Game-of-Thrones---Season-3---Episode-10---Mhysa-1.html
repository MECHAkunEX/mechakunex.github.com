Game of Thrones - Season 3 - Episode 10 - Mhysa (2)20160709
I like the disgust that Bolton has towards Frey. It's interesting
to learn that the Bolton betrayal had been long in the
making. Arya's attack on the Frey guys was awesome. It's cute how
Sansa and Tyrion are getting along. Tyrion's question to Joffrey,
"Kick any puppies lately?" is awesome. Tywin's handling of
Joffrey was awesome. It's cool how Jaime gets to the city. It's
interesting that Stannis and Melissandre change their plans so
quickly. It's a bit transparent that the story of the Rat Cook
was for us as a response to Walder Frey. I'm surprised that
Ygritte really shot Jon.
