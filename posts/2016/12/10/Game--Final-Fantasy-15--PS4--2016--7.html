Game: Final Fantasy 15 (PS4, 2016) (8)20161210
I played three two hour sessions today. It was a lot of fun and I
completed a ton of missions. I explored the whole area that is
unlocked, except for right around Lestallum (I was worried about
automatically getting sucked into the end of the main quest
mission.) I also didn't go to the barricade in the upper left of
the map and didn't go out all the way to the building in the
southern cape. I managed to get two new Royal Arms and was
surprised to be able to beat the Bandersnatch. I generally was
able to fight monsters up to Level 38 or 40-ish. I cashed in my
EXP and went from Level 25 to Level 43 in one go, which was pretty
cool. It worked out to about 190k. I ended up with 275 AP and
spent almost all of it on a bunch of good stuff. I think I will do
some AP grinding at the beginning of the next chapter. This game
is really excellent.
