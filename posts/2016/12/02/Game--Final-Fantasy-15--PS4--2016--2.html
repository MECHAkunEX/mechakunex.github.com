Game: Final Fantasy 15 (PS4, 2016) (3)20161202
I started off by defeating those crabs and then I went to explore
a mine that my son noticed a previous day. I anticipated that
there would be nothing in the mine but a fake front that I
couldn't go it, but it was a big dungeon. We explored it and had a
blast getting surprised by goblins. At the bottom, I found a level
52 big bad boss. I found a place where I couldn't be hit and
cheesed it by shooting it down in about an hour. Right near the
end I rezzed everyone so that they would get the experience and
then I went to the x2 hotel at the port. We got four levels! Nice!
I need to buy more Phoenix Down, especially because I got real
beat up by some level 17 crabs on the beach. I feel like I don't
want to progress the story, but that I'm supposed to and then come
back here later. Annoyingly, even though I beat that boss, I
couldn't get into the tomb because I didn't have the key. I was
really impressed on my way out by the lady that gave a subtle
warning to me that this would happen.
