Movie: X-Men Origins: Wolverine, starring Hugh Jackman (2009)20160307
The opening of this movie is so awesome. There are a lot of weird
things in it though that don't make a lot of sense to me. For
instance, it's not explained how he gets the name "Logan". I like
the cameos and references to the other movies, such as Stryker
talking about his son. Deadpool is great until he inexplicably has
no mouth. It's neat that Cyclops shows up, although it doesn't make
sense that he can't see. This movie has a really bad reputation,
but it really isn't that bad.
