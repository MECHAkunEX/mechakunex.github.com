Game: Dungeons & Dragons - 4th Edition (P&P, 2008)20161001
I played a long campaign of this with some friends in *REDACTED*. I know
that a lot people don't like it, but I really found it awesome. I
love the board game-y/video game-y nature of it and the huge
amount of tactical options.

seemed to get it when I showed him the power cards.

I think it would cool to build a "typical" party and fight every
monster in the original books in a long level-1-to-30 campaign
arc.
