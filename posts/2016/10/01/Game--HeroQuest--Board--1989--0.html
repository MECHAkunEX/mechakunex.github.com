Game: HeroQuest (Board, 1989)20161001
When I was young, I played this a lot and eventually got a lot of
expansions---Kellar's Keep and The Frozen Horror. I remember that
I played it for the first time with my neighbors and then I bought
my own copy and did it all the time. I recently found a copy on
Ebay and bought it.

So far, I've gone through the first few levels with my son as
Zargon. I am using all four characters and I can see that the game
wasn't quite really designed for all four and that it was a fault
to not have the monsters scale with the players. Another design
flaw is that there's basically no reason to not always search
every room for treasure in an attempt to get lots of
resources. All this, combined with my son not yet being a
tactician, makes it a bit of an easy game. I'd really like for him
to want to be the player, but for some reason he doesn't want to.
