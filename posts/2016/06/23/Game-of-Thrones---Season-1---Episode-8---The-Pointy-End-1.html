Game of Thrones - Season 1 - Episode 8 - The Pointy End (2)20160623
Syrio's final scene with Arya was pretty awesome. It is a very
natural way that she becomes independent and starts off her own
journey. Varys is such a complicated guy, but I suppose that my
problem is that I'm looking for heroes. It is really epic the way
the hordes of ravens leave Winterfell. It's interesting that
there is another occurrence of a White Walker. Dany's made such a
huge mistake in this episode that is going to totally change
everything about her life. In many ways, it's similar to the
problems she will cause for herself in Slaver's Bay: disrupting
the old ways caused problems. Joffrey is such a little punk.

I was very curious about why Alliser is such a jerk to Jon
Snow. Apparently the novels reveal that he was a Targaryen
supporter that was forced to the Wall after Robert's Rebellion.
