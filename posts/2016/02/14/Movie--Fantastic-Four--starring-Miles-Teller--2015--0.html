Movie: Fantastic Four, starring Miles Teller (2015)20160214
There were some things I really like about this movie: it was dark
and made becoming transformed a scary experience for the characters
and it really developed the place and setting of the quantum
gate. There were lots of lame things about it though... for
instance, why do they even want to go to this Planet Zero? It's one
thing to just want to explore (that'd be fine) but they seem to
think there's some great resource there. Dr. Doom is very poorly
developed as well. The last act of the movie is too quick and
un-interesting as the characters basically attack twice with their
powers: once where they fail and then once where they
succeed. There's no spectacle and enjoyment of their abilities.

I dislike that there's no explanation of what Reed is doing and no
speculation about how they go their powers or what Planet Zero is.

It's fascinating reading about the reshoots.
