Movie: Blackhat, starring Chris Hemsworth (2015)20160211
It was refreshing to have good and respected Chinese characters as
the heroes of the story. Thor doesn't really pull off being a
hacker and his background is pretty lame. I found the movie very
boring overall and decided to drop it half-way through. There's a
lot of little interesting things throughout, but it doesn't have
enough action to stay hooked and the mystery isn't deep enough to
really want to know what happens. I think the problem is that the
villain just wants to make money and no one's life is really in
danger or anything, so I don't feel any suspense about what is
going to happen. I'd say that the movie is well made, but what they
made wasn't really a good plan to begin with.
