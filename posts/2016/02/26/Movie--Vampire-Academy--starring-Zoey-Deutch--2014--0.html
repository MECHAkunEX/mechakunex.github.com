Movie: Vampire Academy, starring Zoey Deutch (2014)20160226
Going into this movie, I assumed it would be extremely lame and
cliche, like a live action version of the Monster High cartoon. The
movie started off in a really weird way with a lot of action and
proper nouns that made me think it was going to be a sort of
teenage Underworld, but it transformed rapidly into something very
different. After that moment, I thought it would be more like a
cross between Twilight and Harry Potter, but it barely used those
tropes as well. I felt like it was much more like an arc of a
season of Buffy... lots of cute quips and dialog in a really weird
world that is super inspired by pop culture. I really liked it and
feel it might be a personal classic for me. I think I may try to
read the books too, as I got the impression that so much was
compressed for time.
