Movie: Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials, starring Dylan O'Brien (2015)20160226
This movie is way different than the first one: much scarier,
darker, and trying to do a lot more. There were many moments in the
whole story that remind me of Greek mythology. In particular,
there's a long sequence here that reminds me of the Island of the
Lotus-Eaters from the Odyssey. The world is very interesting here
as it mixes together many standard themes from zombie stories and
other tropes of young adult fiction into a new mix. It's strange
how close humans live to the monsters without seemingly any
consequence when they reach the city in the North. I'm surprised in
general about how populated the world is. I found the ending to be
a bit repetitive and I was annoyed that the characters ended
roughly where they began in terms of their goals (although, they've
definitely changed (and earned that change) along the way.)
