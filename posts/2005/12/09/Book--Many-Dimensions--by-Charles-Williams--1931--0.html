Book: Many Dimensions, by Charles Williams (1931)20051209
This is a story about an ancient artifact that is discovered that
grants wishes. It is discovered in early twentieth century
Britain.</p>

<p>The style of the story is interesting. Essentially it is all
dialogue with extremely brief descriptions of the passage of time
and location. As a result of this, the characters are very vivid
and each section of the book reveals more about a single person. It
was a nice change.</p>

<p>Unfortunately, the end of the story is rather anti-climatic and
I felt very unsatisfied by it.</p></div></div>
