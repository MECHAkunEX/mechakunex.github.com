Game: Environmental Station Alpha (Steam, 2015)20151128
I started this game because it has such a distinctive look and I
heard good things about it. Right off the bat, I found it very
annoying how slow the movement is and how bad the loading times
are between screens. Sometimes it is so bad that Windows thinks it
is not responding and the window chrome appears.

Like Castle in the Darkness, it has very bad telegraphing of where
you can go and what you can do. In fact it is so bad that after I
got the grappling hook, I spent over an hour wandering around
trying to get to the next area with no ability to and just a ton
of road-blocks along the way preventing me from going forward. It
is extremely frustrating.
