Game: Castle in the Darkness (Steam, 2015)20151126
My first session with this game was not great. I started with the
Steam Controller and the controls were really bad and almost
impossible to do anything with precision. I switched to the SNES
controller, which was a lot better. Next, I gradually explored the
map. I'm amazed at the poor performance of the game. I get really
bad slow-down at every menu transition, at lots of screen
transitions and during most boss fights. Even with the better
controller, there's still some pain with the "stickiness" of the
character movement. 

I had some frustrating moments where I wasn't sure which was I was
supposed to go and I had two options, but picked the wrong
one---so, I battled my way through a really difficult platforming
segment (including dying and restarting a lot) only to end up in a
room with a locked door. The game clearly harkens to the NES, but
I think is too modern for that to mix well, because in an NES
game, there wouldn't be enough space to have the ability to go in
a bad direction like that. I'm also pretty annoyed by the lack of
a map that I can use to keep track of where I need to go back to.
