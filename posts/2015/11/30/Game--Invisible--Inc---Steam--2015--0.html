Game: Invisible, Inc. (Steam, 2015)20151130
I played two missions of this plus the tutorial. I like it a
lot. It reminds me of a cross between X-COM and Metal Gear Acid,
although I think that so far each of those games does a better job
with being about all action or all stealth. It is good though.

I played with the Steam controller, although it is built for a
mouse and keyboard. I think I am happy with the Steam controller,
because it not an action game, but I had to lower the sensitivity
of the thumb-pad a lot to get it to work nicely. I wish I had a
better sense of what the buttons did, however, because I have to
constantly refer back to them. I also wish the icons were a lot
bigger so they were easier to press.

The opening cutscene strangely stuttered a lot for me and had very
bad performance, as well as audio/visual de-syncing from the
subtitles, which was pretty disappointing.

Overall, I think the game is really rad and I intend to play it a
lot.
