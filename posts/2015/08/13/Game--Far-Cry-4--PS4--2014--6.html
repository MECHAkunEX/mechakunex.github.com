Game: Far Cry 4 (PS4, 2014) (7)20150813
I played today and conquered two fortresses. 

I did the Eastern one almost totally from the sky, but then got
down and shot down a reinforcement helicopter with an RPG. After
that, I only had a single enemy left to take out on the ground.

I did the Western one from the air as well, but it wasn't as
useful because the fortress is so embedded in the mountain. The
big problem is that the reinforcements didn't come until I went
deep enough, so I had to then run out and shoot them down with the
RPG. It worked pretty well. After that, I stood at the top of the
hole and threw grenades and shot my bow down at the enemies. I was
able to do it without being in any real danger.

After that, I did some vehicle escort missions so I could unlock
the ability to buy the Predator sniper rifle. Of course, I don't
have enough money yet, so I'll have figure out so way to get rich
quick. :)

Maybe I will eventually start doing the campaign missions. In
