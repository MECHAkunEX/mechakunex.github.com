Game: Far Cry 4 (PS4, 2014) (2)20150808
I played for a long time today. I did a ton of objectives on the
map but only a few actual missions. Once I got the second outpost
and started opening up the map... it was pretty crazy to see how
big it was and how (in principle) there's no reason I can't just
go anywhere immediately. I suspect that lots of "open world" games
are like this, but I basically never play them, so it is pretty
crazy to me.

I have been spending a lot of time hunting animals to do all the
crafting. Some animals are really annoying to hunt, because it
feels like they never actually spawn where it says that they
will. In particular, rhinos don't seem to spawn and are very hard
to kill. What I really need to hunt down right now are Yaks,
because they stand in the way of a bunch of upgrades.

I like climbing up to something high and jumping off of it. I feel
like the wingsuit and parachute should be automatic though, I
don't always press the button on time.
