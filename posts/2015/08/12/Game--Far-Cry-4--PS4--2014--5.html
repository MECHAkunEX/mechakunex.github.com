Game: Far Cry 4 (PS4, 2014) (6)20150812
Today I went through a bunch of the Shangri-la missions and
started expanding the Ghale Homestead. The Shangri-la missions are
pretty fun and introduce a new way to do the combat which is
enjoyable, although sometimes I just sprint to the next checkpoint
because it is a smarter strategy.

I also took over all the southern outposts. For the two alarm
ones, I used a new technique which is getting in the Buzzer and
raining grenades down from the sky. What a silly, fun way to do
it!

Once I had taken them over, I got access to some Fashion Week
missions, which I did as well. At this point in my crafting, I
just need to get Snow Leopard skins and the rare animals. I'm
annoyed that there are no Snow Leopards in the south.

Finally, I did a few attempts at using the Buzzer to attack the
fortresses, but with no luck. I can't seem to get all that alarms
fast enough and then the attack helicopters take me down. I
presume I just need to find a good angle and
practice. Unfortunately, every time I fail costs money. :(
