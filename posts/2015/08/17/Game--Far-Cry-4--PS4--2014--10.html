Game: Far Cry 4 (PS4, 2014) (11)20150817
I did a bunch of campaign missions and some other missions as they
popped up. On most of the campaign missions, I tried to take a
stealth approach, but then switched to a frontal assault when I
couldn't. When I did the "City of Pain" mission, on the way out, I
used the grenade launcher a lot in my escape, which was pretty
silly. I did the heroin lab one, which was really cleverly done. I
bought all the weapons that I had unlocked because I had such a
huge surplus of money and very little to spend it on.
