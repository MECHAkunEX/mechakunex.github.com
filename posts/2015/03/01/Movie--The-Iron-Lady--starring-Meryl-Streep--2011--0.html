Movie: The Iron Lady, starring Meryl Streep (2011)20150301
I was very interested in learning about Margaret Thatcher, but I
feel like this movie went too quickly over her life. I was hoping
and expecting that the "now" scenes would end quickly and we'd see
a coherent story of her life, but that didn't happen. I suppose it
is "important" to depict the life of people with dementia, but I
would much rather see her rise to power and glory in her
accomplishments.
