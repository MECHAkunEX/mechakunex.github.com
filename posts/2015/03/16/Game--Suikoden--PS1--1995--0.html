Game: Suikoden (PS1, 1995)20150316
I played through the first mission and went over to the town
mentioned in the second one. I don't like the combat... I just did
"Free Will" every round and won. I don't like the seriousness of
the plot. I don't like the way the game looks... I know it isn't
its fault, but I don't like the way it looks like an RPG Maker
game with an ugly and unpolished style. I hear that the story of
the series is amazing, but I guess I needed to have experienced it
when I was younger to appreciate it.
