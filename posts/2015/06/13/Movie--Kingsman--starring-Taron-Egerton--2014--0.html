Movie: Kingsman, starring Taron Egerton (2014)20150613
I like the style of the movie a lot. The cast and acting were
stellar too. The story was neat. I think the language was really
stupidly vulgar for no purpose, unlike American Sniper for instance
it didn't seem like the characters would really talk that way. 

I think a lot of the violence was shot in a really cool way, but
was pointless and not "heroic" violence at all. In particular, the
extended church seen was pretty terrible as we just have to watch
in horror as innocent people are killed by each other and a super
spy. You can't feel good about the spy's skills in a scene like
that.
