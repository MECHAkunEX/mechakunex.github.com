Movie: The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, starring Daniel Craig (2011)20150217
This had a very "James Bond" opening and I was surprised to see
that it had almost the same staff as "Gone Girl".

Very disturbing happenings with the girl. I wonder how she's
connected... she's too young to be Harriett, maybe her daughter? It
is brutal how she gets him back. I feel bad for liking such
vengeance.

Elizabeth is a Biblical name! Will she be next?

The resolution was quite surprising.

It is a very sad ending. But I supposed it is also kind of good
because he was too old for her and she could be sure that he wasn't
abusing her for her money.
