Game: Final Fantasy 10 (HD Remaster) (PS3, 2014)20150117
I started this and played up to the second temple. The story is
much more interesting than I remember it being. It's such a weird
thing to jump forward 1,000 years and see what is different and
what is the same. I find Yuna to be super beautiful and I can tell
my Tidus would like her! I don't remember there being so much
oogling of Lulu's breasts.

I have memories of the combat in this game being very interesting,
but so far it is pretty tame and slow. I'm using the Expert sphere
grid and don't have a good idea yet of what my plan will be.

So far the HD remastering is odd... the character animations
really suffer and a lot of the models have low polygons, so it
looks weird for them to be so crisp and high-res. The areas are
also very good looking, but also look very empty and don't have
the many layers that a more modern game would. I'm also amazed
that there's so much loading. For instance, if you are in a
village, you can go into a building instantly, but coming out gets
a loading screen. Weird!?
