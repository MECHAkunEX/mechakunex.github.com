Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain (PS4, 2015) (6)20151003
I did lots of missions and side-ops today. I finished the Honeybee
mission and completely cleared out the base except for the three
guys that kill the prisoner and go to where the Honeybee is. I
didn't fight the Skulls, because I assumed I was supposed to run
away. I plan on going back and doing the missions again once I can
Fulton larger things. I did a few other side-ops and took over
some bases. Annoyingly, I died on two of those with only two
enemies left. In the first case, they got me because I thought I
was covered after they same me and I blew up. On the other, I
accidentally jump out of a guard tower and fell to my death
because I thought I would land on the outpost wall. Dumb, and
totally my fault, but very annoying that the checkpoint put me
back before I had done anything at all to the base. This game is
so much fun. My only complaint is that it is a bit annoying to
travel around the map when my side-ops are all over the place.
