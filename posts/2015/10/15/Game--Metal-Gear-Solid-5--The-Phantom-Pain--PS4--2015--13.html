Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain (PS4, 2015) (14)20151015
I played a bunch of Africa side-ops missions. I tried to grab an
elite soldier near a tank that was guarded by two snipers and two
guys on patrol. It was a very hard mission. First, the snipers saw
me and got me. Then I tried to get them and the patrol guys
spotted me when I Fulton'd one of the snipers. Then I snuck up on
the tank guy but the tank saw me before I could knock them
out. Then I got spotted while trying to tranq people from
afar... the tank blurted its horn and woke up the ones I had
already got. Finally I did it by getting the snipers, getting one
patrol guy, and then going around and Fultoning the tank. It makes
me think that the Fulton Ballista on the D-Walker would be
awesome.

The mission was particularly challenging because I couldn't have
Quiet with me, since there was no sniper anchor nearby. I've
gotten her up to Bond Level 83. I unlocked the silencer
tranquilizer gun and the Silver outfit. I wonder if the Silver
outfit does anything. I'm getting nervous because I've heard that
you can lose Quiet. She's so awesome, I don't want that.
