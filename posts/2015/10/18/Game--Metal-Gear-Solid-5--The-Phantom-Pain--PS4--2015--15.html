Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain (PS4, 2015) (16)20151018
I played for a long time today. I did side-ops mostly, but I did
do two or three missions in Africa. It is fun to do side-ops, but
I've stopped doing the repeat ones. I hope this won't mean more
won't unlock. I've also stopped running from one location to the
other, and I've been going back to the ACC and then flying
back. Sometimes I will run and do repeat side-op, but I'm not
going to run thousands of meters around Afghanistan anymore.

Quiet is so awesome and powerful. It is kind of ridiculous. They
do a good job making her useless against vehicles, Skulls, etc. I
found the mission where the Skulls were guarding the truck to be
pretty difficult, because I'm not used to doing real fighting.

I got the "Snake is super smelly" scene going back to the Mother
Base. It was really fun. I had 100% Bond with Quiet, so I got the
shower scene with her too, which was pretty awkward.
