Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain (PS4, 2015) (8)20151009
I did a side-op and then I did the mission where you have to
destroy the armored vehicles. I only missed one of them, but I did
miss the prisoner in the van and I didn't get to the prisoner in
the wilderness. It was pretty exciting and fun.

After that mission, I got D-Dog which was pretty
cool. Unfortunately, the next mission I did was going to get
Emmerich. The problem was that I landed the helicopter right over
an armored vehicle, so they shot down the helicopter. I managed to
grab everyone and get through, but it cost a lot of money.

I worked my way up towards the power plant and got into the battle
with Quiet. I shot her with a tranquilizer sniper rifle I had just
developed. I wanted to sneak up on her, but I found it very
difficult, even with D-Dog helping to sniff her out.

I can't believe Miller is such a jerk about her. I suppose it is
based on Huey and Miller's other trust issues. Quiet's opening
scene in the helicopter was definitely pretty cool. I went to
visit her and it was awesome that "Snake Eater" started playing. I
couldn't get Quiet to react to me at all though, I'm not sure what
I'm supposed to do with her to recruit her.
