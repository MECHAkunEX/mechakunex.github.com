Game: Bloodborne (PS4, 2015) (16)20150920
I did all of Yahar'gul, which was very tense because of the Chime
Maidens. I died on my first attack on the One Reborn, but beat it
the next time very quickly. I hid in the stairwell and went out to
do an attack or two. When I was at about 50% health, I used a Bolt
Paper and destroyed it. After that I went to the Orphanage, which
was very tense also because of the Brain Suckers. I beat the
Celestial Emissary on the first try and wasted a Bolt Paper on
it. After that I tried many many time to beat Ebrietas, but kept
dying. Eventually I finally did it by drastically changing my
armor setup, going with the untransformed Holy Blade, and goading
it into the entry hallway so it wouldn't have as much
maneuverability. This was one of the hardest fights I've had so
far. After that I explored the rest of the Lecture Building and
entered the last Nightmare. I think I may be able to beat the game
tomorrow when I play.
