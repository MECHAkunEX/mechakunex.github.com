Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain (PS4, 2015)20150923
I started this and had a blast playing it.

I did the prologue mission and found it very crazy and thought
provoking as to what the game will really be about. I found it
hard to know what I was supposed to do when being chased on the
horse. After the prologue, the game got stuck during the install,
so I had to troubleshoot that, which was annoying.

I did the first mission and took over the first outpost, but not
the second. I like the UI and way that you interact with the game
a lot. I have a strangely huge amount of love for the UI's
voice. She sounds so peaceful and calm. I liked the first mission,
although it felt like I couldn't do anything to get around the
Skulls, but according to the extra mission options, I can.

After that, I did the next mission and tried to Fulton everything
I possibly could, which caused me to spend a long time and get
spotted a lot because I was focusing on that. I think "Fulton
everything" is going to be my MO in this game.

I think I played for too long, but it was nice to get into it a
lot on my playing.
