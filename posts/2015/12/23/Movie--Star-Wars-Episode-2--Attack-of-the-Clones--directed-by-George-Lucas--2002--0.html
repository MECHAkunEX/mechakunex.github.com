Movie: Star Wars Episode 2: Attack of the Clones, directed by George Lucas (2002)20151223
This movie is way better than Phantom Menace.

I vastly prefer the droids as the comic relief characters than
Jar-Jar. They are much more about "dad jokes" whereas Jar-Jar is
more like fart and poop jokes.

Portman looks so good in this movie. Her dresses are so cool and
they are all so different and interesting. The cute montage of
Anakin and Amidala worked very well. It is odd that no one comments
on them kissing multiple times during the Jedi fight in the arena.

I think my favorite part of the movie is how gradually, slowly, and
believable things are turning bad for the republic and Anakin.

It is very impactful to see the clones and the star destroyers
loaded up and off for the first time. The whole plan of Palpatine
and Dooku is very interesting. I feel like I need to study the
Wookipedia to know what happened during the Clone Wars TV show.
