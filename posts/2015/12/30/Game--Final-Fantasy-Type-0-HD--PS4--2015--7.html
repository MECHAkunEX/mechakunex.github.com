Game: Final Fantasy Type-0 HD (PS4, 2015) (8)20151230
After leaving it running for several nights and most of a day, I
farmed enough SPP to unlock the SP Crystal. I had Rem as the
active character, so now she is super powerful as well. It's
pretty rad. I continued doing missions and got to
Chapter 7. Walking around across the world to do everything is
very painful. I'm trying to get the airship, which exacerbates the
problem because you've got to cover a lot of ground to get to the
Expert Trials related to it.

The missions between Chapters 5 and 7 were really awesome. I
didn't understand what it meant when I divided up my group, so I
stupidly didn't include some of my favorite and valuable
characters in the group that I actually had to control and use. I
managed, however. Then in the next part, it was funny to not be
able to use Rem because she is so powerful for me, although
clearly the reason why was the story. I beat Gilgamesh on the
Bridge, although I got the feeling he was supposed to win.
