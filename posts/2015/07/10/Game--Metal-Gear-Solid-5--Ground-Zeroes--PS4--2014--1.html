Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: Ground Zeroes (PS4, 2014) (2)20150710
I played for about an hour. I had to repeat my approach to the
prison area where Chico was, which was pretty easy after having
done it once, although I think I didn't do it as efficiently as I
could have. Once I got him I went over slowly to where Paz was. I
got spotted as soon as I went underground because I was being
sloppy and didn't case the situation enough, despite having
interrogated a guard as to where other guards would be. I managed
to get away and then hide over a period of time. I did have to
fight off some people, but I managed to circle around an obstacle
in just the right way as to avoid them. It was kind of silly that
I wasn't caught because I was standing right in front of where Paz
was... a real person would of course know what was up, but I
appreciate when they make the game fun!
