Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: Ground Zeroes (PS4, 2014)20150708
I played this for about an hour. It looks and sounds amazing. I am
very impressed with the sound particularly. I thought that I would
dislike Keifer, and while I don't like him, I like Hayter way more
and feel like he'd talk more if it was Hayter.

The gameplay seems awesome. I tried to shoot a bunch of fools from
where you land and didn't do a good job and hid out, using Reflex
to kill guards as they came up. Then, I slowly made my way to
where the prisoners are. I had a lot of fun sneaking around. I
feel like I could play a game like this for a long time. It's
great.

I listened to most of the tapes you get in the beginning and liked
it a lot. It's fun to have this be my first PS4 title.
