Game: Metal Gear Solid 5: Ground Zeroes (PS4, 2014) (5)20150714
I played the mission again and was more meticulous. I went through
and marked every enemy, killed a lot of them, and got through with
no alerts. I had a C rank, however, because of Reflex shots and
more importantly, a very high time to completion. I don't think I
will do it again for an S-rank, unless it is needed to get another
tape. I'm not sure what I would really do differently if I did it
again. So far, I'm at about a dollar per hour of fun and I still
need to do the other missions, so I think I'm going to be okay
with my investment.

I did get another tape that had an Interrogation where Skullface
seems to rip off Paz's clothes and talks about her body to Chico
and tells Chico to rape her. Yikes. That's intense and
disturbingly unnecessary. I suspect that Skullface is really Zero
or someone else in Cipher.
