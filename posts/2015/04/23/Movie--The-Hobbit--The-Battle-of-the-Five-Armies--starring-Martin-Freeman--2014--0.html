Movie: The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies, starring Martin Freeman (2014)20150423
This was a nice finish to the Hobbit movies. I thought that it was
a bit goofy when they showed the dwarf king being obsessed with
gold. I believe he would be, but I think it was shot and acted in a
goofy way. The final battle was pretty epic. Although, the thing I
like most about the Middle Earth movies is when the Hobbits and
other weak creatures do great and brave things... this movie didn't
really have any of that because everything important was done by
super-heroes.
