Game: Penny Arcade's On the Rain-Slick Precipice of Darkness - Episode 3 (XBLIG, 2012) (3)20121227
I finished the game this afternoon after shoveling snow. In the
last part of the game, I stopped balancing my classes and just
focused on a strategy that I knew could win. My strategy was so
effective, I was able to defeat the final boss in a single hit
after a few rounds of preparation.

Overall, I found the game okay. The combat was too easy, but a
little bit stressful to not be totally boring. (I never died
during the whole game.) The writing was very funny in the
beginning, but got increasingly crazy and out-of-whack. I like
the weirdness, but I didn't really like it when it was trying to
be "dark" or "edgy".

I tried to do the Prologue, but (a) I was annoyed that I had to
go back to level 1, (b) I didn't know anything about the previous
games, and (c) the non-sense story had gotten on my nerves.

The game is definitely worth the price!
