Game: Bangai-O HD: Missile Fury (XBLA, 2011) (6)20121231
This morning I played for about two hours.

Stage 34 --- This stage was fairly difficult, because it is quite
a bit different than normal levels. I was able to finish it by
shooting missiles while pushing the blocks to destroy the turrets
and charge up another Counter for when both blocks are in
place. Then it was just a matter of kiting a little.

Stage 35 --- This stage is ridiculously hard. I haven't beat it
yet and I played it for over an hour. It took a long time to get
to the big bosses at the end, but I haven't been able to get them
yet. When I watched a video of someone beating it... it seems so
easy but I can't seem to do it. I'll have to try something else.
