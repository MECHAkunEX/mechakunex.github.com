Game: Dragon Quest 4: Chapters of the Chosen (DS, 2007) (6)20120427
I played for about three hours and did a lot of exploring and
learning about the many quests I need to finish to get all the
Zenithian equipment. There's a lot to keep track of and
remember. I feel like I need to write it down. I think in a modern
game there would be in-game markers of some kind that might make
it to easy or feel to "fetch questy", but when you actually have
to remember what's going on, it feels more real and fun. It's an
interesting thing.

- I have the water soaking stone
- I need to go to a mine near the French stuff
