Book: The House of the Lord, by James E. Talmage (1912)20070810
<p>A classic, although I was not terribly impressed. The book is
very useful as evidence that the general layout, purpose, and
outward form of the temple ordinances are not secret. I suppose it
would be a fabulous introduction to the *REDACTED* conception of
temple worship for a non-*REDACTED*, or recent convert, but it does
not have the meat I anticipated. Nevertheless, I cannot fault it
for not being what it never intended to be.</p>
