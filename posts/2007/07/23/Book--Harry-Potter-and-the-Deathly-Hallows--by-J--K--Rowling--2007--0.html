Book: Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, by J. K. Rowling (2007)20070723
<p>I was hoping very much for a different ending... more
sinister (particularly given the growing wickedness of our
protagonist... pp 435, 508 and 531), but I was happy with
what we got, although the epilogue was a bit cheese-ball,
and worrisome about potential sequels in the Wizarding
world.</p>

<p>I thought there were some great innuendos in the book
(pp. 52, 113), great cleverness (pp. 97, 125--126), humour
(p. 140), sinisterness (p. 242), absurd <em>deus ex
machina</em> (Chapter 19.)</p>

<p>I hope I'm not the only one to realize that page 328
allows us to place the timing of all these events.</p>

<p>I thought page 355 was a bit absurd given that is
essentially a random part of the "book."</p>

<p>A recurring thought was how great the movie will be
given the enormous amount of action in this
movie. Hopefully it will be like <cite>Kill Bill</cite>:
two parts.</p>
