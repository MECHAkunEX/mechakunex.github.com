Book: The Lovely Bones, by Alice Sebold (2002)20070731
<p>In the first few pages of this book, Susie is raped and
murdered. The story recounts how she watches her family grow and
crumble and grow from heaven. It is excruciating. A particularly
horrible moment is in the week after, her sister starts working out
intensely, as if to be prepared if she is ever attacked. The book
just makes you want to rip out your heart as you feel so much pain
for these people and despise them for their choices. So sad.</p>
